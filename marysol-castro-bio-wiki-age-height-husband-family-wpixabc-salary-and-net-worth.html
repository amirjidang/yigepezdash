Marysol Castro Biography

Marysol Castro is a famous American public address announcer at Citi Field, home of the New York Mets as well as a broadcast journalist.She was born and brought up in Bronx, New York, United States.

 Previously, She was employed as a news anchor at WPIX in New York, weather forecaster for The Early Show on CBS in the year 2011. She also featured a correspondent for the weekend edition of Good Morning America on ABC from 2004 to 2010. Castro started working at New Haven-based WTNH-TV in June 2015, to replace for weekday morning traffic reports and anchor up to mid-November 2015. In January 2019, Castro joined the booth of the Android as well as iOS app Ballpark DJ making it possible for any youth softball or baseball team to be broadcasted by her professional voice.
