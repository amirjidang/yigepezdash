Barton Hills – The Richest City in Michigan

Barton Hills, a village within Ann Arbor Charter Township, holds the coveted title of the richest city in Michigan. With a median household income surpassing $250,000 per year and an average household income of $421,909, it stands out as an affluent municipality in the state. Barton Hills is one of only two cities in Michigan where the median home values exceed $1 million, reflecting the opulence and exclusivity of the area.

Residents of Barton Hills also pay substantial property taxes, with the median amount exceeding $10,000 per year. This high tax rate enables the village to provide exceptional public services and maintain its pristine surroundings. From stunning homes to picturesque landscapes, Barton Hills exudes luxury and prosperity.

With its unparalleled wealth and breathtaking scenery, Barton Hills epitomizes the epitome of luxury living in Michigan.

Lake Angelus and Bloomfield Hills – Other Wealthy Cities in Michigan

Aside from Barton Hills, there are two other wealthy cities in Michigan worth exploring – Lake Angelus and Bloomfield Hills. While they may not top the list in terms of median household income, both cities still boast impressive wealth and offer an exceptional standard of living.

Lake Angelus, a suburb of Detroit, is known for its picturesque lakeside setting and serene atmosphere. With a median household income of $191,667, Lake Angelus secures the second-place ranking among Michigan’s richest cities. What sets it apart is its higher mean household income of $376,000 and a median home value of $1.2 million. The city’s residents enjoy a peaceful environment and the beauty of nature, making it an ideal choice for those seeking luxury and tranquility.

On the other hand, Bloomfield Hills, with its manufacturing industry and high-income management occupations, ranks third in terms of wealth. The city boasts a median household income of $176,136, and a mean household income of $393,000. Additionally, the median home value in Bloomfield Hills is around $835,000. Its charm lies in the perfect blend of natural beauty and architectural elegance, making it an attractive destination for affluent individuals and families.

Both Lake Angelus and Bloomfield Hills have high median property taxes, with homeowners paying in excess of $10,000 per year. However, these taxes are a small price to pay for the quality of life, safety, and amenities that these cities offer their residents.

Orchard Lake Village – Wealth and Lower Property Taxes

Orchard Lake Village, located in the northwest suburbs of Detroit, is a distinguished city that ranks fourth among the wealthiest cities in Michigan. Although it has a smaller population compared to the top three cities, Orchard Lake Village stands out for its unique combination of wealth and lower property taxes.

While other affluent cities in Michigan may have higher median household incomes, Orchard Lake Village offers homeowners a financial advantage with median property taxes paid below $10,000 per year. In fact, the median property taxes in Orchard Lake Village are approximately $8,258 per year, making it an appealing choice for those seeking a luxurious lifestyle without the burden of excessive taxes.

In addition to its favorable property tax rates, Orchard Lake Village boasts a tranquil and picturesque setting, with its beautiful lakes and tree-lined streets. Residents of this thriving community enjoy the benefits of affluent living, including high-end shopping and dining experiences, exceptional schools, and access to a variety of recreational activities.

With its unique combination of wealth and lower property taxes, Orchard Lake Village continues to be a sought-after destination for those looking to enjoy a prosperous lifestyle in the state of Michigan.

Birmingham – Affluence in the Largest City

Birmingham, located in Oakland County, is the largest city in terms of households among the top 10 richest cities in Michigan. Despite having a lower median household income compared to the top cities, Birmingham still boasts an incredibly high median income of $137,907. The average household income is even higher at $219,420. The city’s high incomes can be attributed, in part, to its significant employment in the manufacturing industry and management occupations.

With its affluent reputation, Birmingham offers residents a wide range of amenities and attractions. The city is known for its upscale shopping destinations, fine dining establishments, and luxurious residential neighborhoods. Birmingham’s vibrant downtown area is filled with art galleries, boutiques, and entertainment venues, providing residents with a high-quality urban experience.

Furthermore, Birmingham’s commitment to education is evident with its top-rated public and private schools. The city’s emphasis on providing quality education contributes to its desirability among families seeking a prosperous and enriching environment for their children.

University of Michigan – A Driving Force

The University of Michigan is a key driver of Ann Arbor’s economy and contributes to the city’s reputation as one of the wealthiest in Michigan. With its world-class academic programs, research facilities, and renowned medical center, the university attracts top talent and generates high-paying jobs. This influx of educated professionals, coupled with the university’s influence on the local culture, plays a significant role in shaping Ann Arbor’s affluence.

In addition to its economic impact, the University of Michigan offers a range of educational and cultural opportunities for the community. The university’s museums, galleries, and performing arts centers provide residents with access to world-class exhibitions, performances, and lectures. Ann Arbor’s residents benefit from the intellectual stimulation and cultural enrichment that the university fosters.

In conclusion, Ann Arbor stands out among Michigan’s wealthiest cities due to the presence of the University of Michigan. This university town offers a unique blend of academic excellence, cultural vibrancy, and natural beauty, making it an attractive place to live for both students and professionals seeking a high quality of life.

Plymouth and Novi – Affluence and Ideal Locations

Plymouth and Novi are two affluent cities in Michigan known for their prime locations. Both cities offer a desirable balance of urban amenities and natural beauty, making them highly sought-after areas for residents and visitors alike.

Why Plymouth is a Prime Location

Plymouth, situated between Detroit and Ann Arbor, is a charming city with a rich history and a vibrant downtown area. The city boasts a range of local parks, providing ample opportunities for outdoor activities and relaxation. Some of the notable parks in Plymouth include Kellogg Park, which hosts various festivals and events throughout the year, and Maybury State Park, offering miles of scenic trails and picturesque landscapes.

Plymouth’s downtown area is bustling with local shops, boutiques, restaurants, and entertainment venues, creating a lively and inviting atmosphere. The city’s commitment to historic preservation is evident in its well-preserved architecture and unique character, which adds to its appeal.

What Makes Novi a Desirable Destination

Located between Detroit and Ann Arbor, Novi is a rapidly growing city that offers a wealth of lakes, golf courses, and outdoor adventures. The city’s abundant lakes provide residents with opportunities for boating, fishing, and other water activities. Some of the popular lakes in Novi include Walled Lake, Wixom Lake, and Commerce Lake.

Novi is also known for its thriving commercial centers, including Twelve Oaks Mall, one of Michigan’s largest shopping destinations. The city’s vibrant dining scene offers a wide range of culinary experiences, from casual eateries to upscale restaurants. In addition, Novi hosts various community events and festivals throughout the year, fostering a strong sense of community and providing residents with ample entertainment options.

With their prime locations, rich amenities, and high standards of living, it’s no wonder that Plymouth and Novi are considered two of the most affluent cities in Michigan.

Traverse City – Wealth in Northern Michigan

Traverse City, located in northern Michigan, is known for its stunning natural beauty, vibrant culture, and thriving economy. With its high median income and flourishing industries, Traverse City stands out as one of the wealthiest cities in northern Michigan.

Traverse City’s economy is driven by various sectors, including tourism, agriculture, and manufacturing. The city attracts visitors from all over the country who come to explore the breathtaking landscapes of Sleeping Bear Dunes National Lakeshore, indulge in the region’s renowned cherry orchards, and enjoy the vibrant downtown area filled with local boutiques and restaurants.

In terms of median income, Traverse City ranks among the top cities in Michigan. The high earning potential in the area allows residents to enjoy a comfortable lifestyle and contribute to the city’s overall prosperity. The combination of picturesque surroundings, a thriving economy, and a strong sense of community makes Traverse City an attractive destination for those seeking wealth and a high quality of life.
