The Most Expensive Land Rover Cars In The World, Ranked

Quick Links

  • 2023 Range Rover P530 SV LWB - $218,300

  • 2014 Range Rover Holland & Holland - $285,000
  • 2023 Range Rover SV Carmel Edition - $345,000

Land Rover is a renowned British automotive brand synonymous with luxury, performance, and unparalleled off-road capability. Over the years, Land Rover has consistently pushed the boundaries of automotive engineering, crafting vehicles that embody a perfect blend of opulence and adventure. Within their extensive lineup, there are a select few Land Rover models that stand out for their sheer exclusivity and astronomical price tags. This article delves into the realm of opulence and explores the world of the most expensive Land Rover cars.

2021 Range Rover Plug-in Hybrid - $104,900

The 2021 Range Rover Plug-in Hybrid combines the robustness of a gasoline engine with the efficiency of an electric motor. Propelled by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generating 296 horsepower, alongside a 114-horsepower electric motor, this hybrid powertrain harmoniously produces 398 horsepower and 472 lb-ft of torque, delivering abundant power and swift acceleration. With an electric range of up to 19 miles, the vehicle can exclusively run on electric power, and recharging it using a Level 2 charger takes less than 3 hours. Safety is paramount in the 2021 Range Rover Plug-in Hybrid, as it incorporates a range of advanced safety features.

2017 Range Rover Supercharged - $121,600

The 2017 Range Rover Supercharged is an elegant and sophisticated SUV that exudes confidence with its distinctive grille, LED headlights, and dynamic posture. Enhanced by its 21-inch wheels, this SUV commands attention and presence on the road. Among the standout features of the 2017 Range Rover Supercharged are its panoramic sunroof, power-adjustable seats, heated and ventilated front seats, rearview camera, blind-spot monitoring, lane departure warning, and automatic emergency braking. Additionally, it boasts a 10.2-inch touchscreen display with navigation, Bluetooth connectivity, and a premium sound system, providing an elevated driving experience.

2022 Range Rover Sport SVR Carbon Edition - $138,500

The Carbon Edition represents an enhancement to the already remarkable Range Rover Sport SVR, showcasing exclusive exterior and interior design elements. A standout characteristic of the Carbon Edition is its extensive utilization of carbon fiber in the vehicle's exterior composition. Notably, the SUV boasts an exposed carbon fiber bonnet, while lightweight carbon fiber is employed for the hood, grille, fender vents, mirror caps, and tailgate finisher. This not only bestows the vehicle with a distinct aesthetic appeal but also contributes to reduced overall weight. The car offers a variety of color options, including Firenze Red, Estoril Blue, Fuji White, and Sanguinello Orange, allowing for personalized style preferences.

2023 Range Rover First Edition - $169,400

The 2023 Range Rover First Edition represents the epitome of opulent SUVs, showcasing the finest in British engineering and design. As the inaugural edition of the latest Range Rover generation, it introduces a host of advanced features and state-of-the-art technology. With a sleek and refined exterior design, the Range Rover First Edition presents a contemporary interpretation of the iconic Range Rover silhouette. It exudes elegance and sophistication, complemented by 22-inch alloy wheels, a panoramic sunroof, matrix LED headlights, and a power gesture tailgate, accentuating its allure. The interior of the cabin is adorned in luxurious premium leather, showcasing exquisite stitching and piping details.

2023 Range Rover P530 SV LWB - $218,300

The 2023 Range Rover P530 SV LWB embodies luxury and power, delivering an extraordinary driving experience. This SUV combines elegance with high performance, featuring all-wheel drive and accommodating four doors and five seats, ensuring ample comfort and space for passengers. Designed with passenger comfort in mind, the P530 SV LWB offers generous room for up to five individuals. The seats are adorned in exquisite premium leather upholstery and equipped with heating and cooling functionalities, enhancing the overall luxurious experience.

2021 Range Rover SV Autobiography Ultimate Edition - $250,000

The Range Rover SV Autobiography Ultimate Edition of 2021 is available in two variations: a long-wheelbase version and a shorter 'Dynamic' version with a shorter wheelbase. This exceptional vehicle showcases an eye-catching design, characterized by its Orchard Green body color, Narvik black roof and mirrors, copper accents, and 22-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels. The interior of the Range Rover SV Autobiography Ultimate Edition of 2021 is equally remarkable, boasting a variety of luxurious leather upholstery choices. In the long-wheelbase version, drivers can enjoy reclining rear seats reminiscent of those found in airline cabins, along with an integrated Zenith timepiece placed in the center console.

2014 Range Rover Holland & Holland - $285,000

This exclusive model is the result of a collaboration between two iconic British brands, Land Rover and Holland & Holland. The 2014 model was built upon the foundation of the Range Rover Autobiography Black, and subsequently, the two brands created special-edition vehicles based on other Range Rover models. The Range Rover Holland & Holland edition stands out with its distinctive color scheme, combining a deep green hue with a rich dark chocolate brown, drawing inspiration from the signature colors of Holland & Holland's bespoke shotguns. Additionally, this car showcases exclusive badging and trim, including chrome-plated door handles and a grille proudly branded with the Holland & Holland logo.

2023 Range Rover SV Carmel Edition - $345,000

The 2023 Range Rover SV Carmel Edition stands as Land Rover's most expensive vehicle ever produced. This ultra-exclusive and coveted SUV epitomizes the pinnacle of Land Rover's excellence in luxury and performance. With a substantial price tag of $345,000, only 17 units of this car have been crafted exclusively for the North American market. The luxury car showcases a captivating Satin Bronze exterior, perfectly complemented by 23-inch diamond-turned wheels adorned with matching Satin Bronze accents. Enhancing its allure, the vehicle is equipped with SV Bespoke puddle lamps, illuminating the ground at night and further adding to its overall appeal. While the exterior undoubtedly catches the eye, it is within the interior of the Carmel Edition where this vehicle truly dazzles.
