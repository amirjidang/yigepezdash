How Does Georgia Groome Feel About Aaron Taylor-Johnson's Marriage?

Aaron Taylor-Johnson and Sam Taylor-Johnson got married in 2012 and still seem to be very content together. He was 22 at the time and she was 45, which got people talking.

Since it can be tricky to see a former flame move on with someone new, does that mean that the actor's rumored ex-girlfriend Georgia Groome thought that the age difference was a bad idea?

Groome doesn't seem to have shared her opinion publicly. If she does think that the age difference is too much, she has never said so, and fans can only speculate about what she might be thinking.

If the actors were together in 2008, then they were both young at the time, and they might be fine with moving on from that part of their lives. Groome and Taylor-Johnson might have both decided not to talk about their exes.

According to The List, Taylor-Johnson and Groome are rumored to have dated from 2008 to 2009. They starred in the movie Angus, Thongs and Perfect Snogging together.

Their characters, Georgia Nicolson and Robbie Jennings, are romantically involved. The movie came out in 2008 and was based on the book series by Louise Rennison. When Taylor-Johnson starred in the film, it was pretty early on in his career, and he was credited as Aaron Taylor as he hadn't married Sam Taylor-Johnson yet.

While Taylor-Johnson and Groome never seemed to talk about whether they were really together or not, his career began heating up shortly after they starred in their movie together. It would only be a matter of time before everyone would be talking about him.

In 2009, the actor played John Lennon in Nowhere Boy and Dave Lizewski/Kick-A** in Kick-A**, two roles that brought him attention. Movies like Bullet Train, A Million Little Pieces, Godzilla, and Avengers: Age of Altron cemented him as an actor to watch.

Groome has worked on a consistent basis in Hollywood, but her projects haven't been as well-known. From 2013 to 2015, she played Emily in the BBC sitcom Up the Women. The show aired for two seasons and focused on a group of women trying to get the right to vote.

Who Is Georgia Groome Dating Now?

If Georgia Groome's name is familiar to Harry Potter fans, that's because she is Rupert Grint's long-time partner. According to People, they are parents to their daughter Wednesday G., who was born in May 2020. The relationship started in 2011.

The couple doesn't share a lot about their romance, which could also explain why Groome doesn't seem to talk about her dating life or her ex-boyfriends. Grint has opened up a few times, though.

Who Did Aaron Taylor-Johnson Date Before Marrying Sam Taylor-Johnson?

According to The List, people think that Aaron Taylor-Johnson was in a relationship with Alice Connor from 2006 to 2007. The two appeared in the movie The Thief Lord together. Neither one ever confirmed it, however.

While the actor stayed quiet about his rumored past relationships, he has definitely spoken out about his marriage. It's possible to think that it's frustrating for him to receive criticism about the person who he has chosen to spend his life with.
