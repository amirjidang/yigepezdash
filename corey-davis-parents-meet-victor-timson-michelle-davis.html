Corey Davis Parents: Meet Victor Timson, Michelle Davis

Corey Davis is an American football wide receiver for the New York Jets of the National Football League. He played college football at Western Michigan, where he became the NCAA Division I FBS leader in career receiving yards.

Corey Davis Parents: Meet Victor Timson,  Michelle Davis

Victor Timson and Michelle Davis are the parents of the wide receiver for the New York Jets Corey Davis.

Mr. Victor Timson and his wife Michelle Davis share seven children including Corey Davis and Titus Davis.

Aside from this, Corey’s parents other details are not known.

But their son Corey Davis attended Wheaton Warrenville South High School in Wheaton, Illinois.

As a true freshman at Western Michigan in 2013, Davis set school freshman records with 67 receptions for 941 yards and was named the Mid-American Conference (MAC) Freshman of the Year.

Corey was drafted by the Tennessee Titans fifth overall in the 2017 NFL Draft.

On May 1, 2020, the Titans declined the fifth-year option on Davis’ contract, making him a free agent in 2021.
