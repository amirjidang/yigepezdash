Coco Austin Gets Vajazzled With Us: Watch

Bling bling, baby! Coco Austin is trying out the hottest beauty trends for Us Weekly Video in our brand-new exclusive digital series Out of This World With Coco Austin. Watch as Coco gets vajazzled in our second episode, as seen in the video above!

The Ice Loves Coco alum, 37, accompanied Us to New York City’s Brazil Bronze Soho, where she bejeweled her lady bits.

“Today, I’m gonna be getting my hoo-ha blinged out!” she tells Us before her treatment. “You know I like bling. You know I like sparkle, so let’s go check this out.”

After looking at several rhinestone configurations, Coco eventually decides on a dazzling, vibrant pink design to glue onto her skin just above her privates. After seeing the cluster of gems bursting near her bikini line, the “Shoe Freak” singer decides she needs even more sparkle.

“So this isn’t that low. I’m sure everybody goes lower, right? But this is — I can share it with everyone. Yay!” she enthuses. “Oh my goodness, I wanna put more on! … Can I have more vajazzle, please, darling?”

Asked how her husband, Ice T, will react to her newly glittered nether regions, Coco replies: “Ice isn’t really into bling all that much, but I think after he sees this, he’ll be like, ‘Yeah, that’s kinda cute, honey.’”

She adds: “I mean, it’s not gonna turn him on. I can guarantee that. The only thing that will turn him on is just a pair of heels. But you never know. This might tweak something!”
