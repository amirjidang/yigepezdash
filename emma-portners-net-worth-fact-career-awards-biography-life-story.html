Emma Portner is A Canadian professional Choreographer and Dancer. Already well known and respected in the dance world, she has recently become more recognized after marrying actress Ellen Page.

Famous For

  • A Canadian professional Dancer, Choreographer, and Dance Teacher.
  • For her work on Justin Bieber’s ‘Life is Worth Living’ in 2015.

Early Life of Emma Portner

Emma was born in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. Her birth sign is Sagittarius. She began dancing since her early childhood. She was the only daughter of her mother Heather and father Dan Portner. Furthermore, she has two brothers named Dylan and Quinn. She is Canadian by nationality and belongs to white caucasian descent.
Talking about her education, She went to St. Peter High School in her early teenage days and graduated with honors in dance from the Canterbury High School in 2012. Then she attended Ailey School throughout 2013.

Career of Emma Portner

She started dancing in Ottawa at Leeming Danceworks before spending her summers with The National Ballet of Canada. She moved to New York City to train at the Ailey School when she was 16.
Emma Portner choreographed for Jim Steinman’s ‘Bat Out of Hell The Musical’. She has her own Dance Company called ‘Flock’d’. She teaches dance in New York, Canada, and Los Angeles. She also happens to be the youngest woman to ever choreograph a musical, ‘A Bat Out Of Hell.’ This musical was shown in Toronto and London’s West End. Her hard work has taken her in the high level of the dancing field.
Portner posted a YouTube video of her dance which garnered 50 million views and ultimately got her casting in Justin Bieber’s video. She choreographed and starred in Justin’s Bieber’s music video for his song ‘Life is Worth Living’ in 2015 and she also choreographed for Bieber’s ‘Purpose World Tour’.
Recently, she is busy with her dancing classes at Broadway Dance Center. Her classes are focused on contemporary choreography. Also, she teaches how to dance emotionally and expand the body limits.

Awards and Achievements of Emma Portner

  • In 2012, she was awarded the American Dance Award for Young Choreographer of the Year.
  • In October 2012, she released a collaborative short dancing film, ‘Dancing in the Dark’, with fellow dancer Matt Luck. The video has more than 550,000 views.
