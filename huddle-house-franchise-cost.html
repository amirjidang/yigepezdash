About Huddle House Franchise

Huddle House is an iconic American diner franchise known for its round-the-clock breakfast, Southern hospitality, and the cherished tradition of classic American dining. With over 50 years in business, Huddle House has become a beloved gathering place in hometowns across the United States.

At Huddle House, the breakfast menu takes center stage, accounting for more than half of the sales. With a commitment to serving delicious meals at any time of day, Huddle House offers a wide variety of crowd-pleasing breakfast options that keep customers coming back for more.

What truly sets Huddle House apart is its embodiment of Southern hospitality. From the warm greeting upon entering to the friendly and attentive service throughout the meal, Huddle House franchisees uphold the values of genuine hospitality that make customers feel like part of a close-knit community.

Huddle House has successfully established itself as an iconic brand that captures the essence of the classic American dining experience. Its welcoming atmosphere, crave-worthy comfort food, and commitment to exceptional service have made it a staple in small towns, suburbs, and exurbs across the country.

Franchise Requirements

If you’re considering becoming a Huddle House franchise owner, there are specific requirements that you need to meet. These requirements ensure that you have the necessary financial resources and qualifications to run a successful franchise. Here are the key franchise requirements:

  • Financial Requirements: To invest in a Huddle House franchise, you must have a minimum liquid capital of $100,000 to $100,000. This capital will be used to cover various startup and operational costs.
  • Franchise Fee: The exact franchise fee for Huddle House is not specified. However, it’s worth noting that the franchise offers a $5,000 discount on the first unit agreement for veterans, providing them with additional financial support.
  • New Development Investment: The total investment required for new development ranges from $444,192 to $1,550,670. This investment covers expenses such as construction, equipment, and initial inventory.
  • Resale Investment: For those interested in purchasing an existing Huddle House franchise, the resale investment ranges from $112,480 to $657,810. This investment amount may vary based on factors such as the location and current financial performance of the franchise.

It’s important to note that these figures are subject to change, and it’s always recommended to verify the current franchise fees and investment amounts with the Huddle House franchise directly. Additionally, the franchise requirements may vary depending on individual circumstances and the availability of territories.
