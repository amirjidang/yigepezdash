Inside Megan Fox's Relationship With MGK's 13-Year-Old Daughter Casie

Highlights

  • Machine Gun Kelly's 13-year-old daughter Casie gets along well with and adores Megan Fox and her kids.
  • Megan Fox also has a positive relationship with MGK's daughter and has been seen hanging out with her and Kelly in various places.
  • Machine Gun Kelly speaks lovingly about his daughter and hopes she maintains her innocence as she grows older. He even includes her in the musical process and trusts her opinion.

Machine Gun Kelly's 13-year-old daughter Casie, tends to stay out of the spotlight but has recently been spotted with her rapper father and his on/off again partner Megan Fox.

Machine Gun Kelly shares his daughter Casie Colson Baker with his ex-girlfriend, Emma Cannon. The pair dated as teenagers and welcomed their daughter the same year they met.

According to sources, Machine Gun Kelly already gets along well with Megan’s kids, and the feeling is mutual. “Her kids Bodhi, Journey, and Noah think he’s super cool,” a source told Life & Style in November 2021. “MGK’s daughter, Casie, simply adores Megan and her kids,” the source added.

Machine Gun Kelly met Megan Fox on the set of the indie film, Midnight in the Switchgrass, and they became engaged in January 2022. In February 2023, break-up rumors started to surface after Megan posted cryptic lyrics about infidelity, and then deactivated her Instagram account.

Since these social media posts, the pair have been seen together, although they have yet to reveal their relationship status.

Megan Fox's Relationship With Machine Gun Kelly's Daughter

Megan Fox's appears to have a positive relationship with Machine Gun Kelly's daughter, Casie Colson Baker. Fox knows a thing or two about being a step-parent. The Jennifer's Body star was also stepmom to Green's son Kassius Lijah Marcil-Green, from his previous relationship with actress Vanessa Marcil.

In February last year, the actress posted snaps of the pair hanging out with Kelly to her Instagram Stories. They appeared to be having fun together at the 27 Club Coffee in Ohio.

In June 2021, the family was seen having dinner together in Venice, California. They were also photographed outside of Wild Rooftop Concert, where Fox was seen walking hand-in-hand with Kelly, who had his other arm wrapped around his daughter's shoulders.

Machine Gun Kelly's Relationship With His Daughter

The musician and actor spoke to Drew Barrymore on her talk show about his love for his teenage daughter and how she is growing up before his very eyes.

"I was just walking behind my daughter in the airport the other day and she just has this walk, it's this pure bounce, she's so excited for life like she's so young in her life," Machine Gun Kelly explained.

He also explained how he hopes she maintains her innocence as she grows older. "I've experienced so much in my life. I just pray to every God that exists that she just keeps that bounce forever and that no one interferes with that." The "Bad Things" singer added that he hopes he "always get[s] to stand back and watch her have that bounce."

"I will take any amount of torture that would come her way if it can just be on me so that she can keep that forever because… it's worth living for to see that. Her voice is so sweet and it's not tainted with what the world has to offer," he said.

He also includes his daughter in the musical process. "I trust her opinion more than I trust my own now," he told Kelly Clarkson on her talk show. "Like, she has her finger on the pulse of what's hot, or if I'm doing a song and it's the right one, she'll confirm it."

Megan Fox And Machine Gun Kelly's Blended Family

Machine Gun Kelly isn't the only parent in this relationship, Megan Fox co-parents her three children — Noah Shannon Green, Bodhi Ransom Green, and Journey River Green — with her ex-husband Brian Austin Green.

“The kids get along great, which is huge,” an insider told Life & Style “Even Brian, who honestly didn’t think Megan and MGK would last, is fine with his kids’ future stepdad.”

According to the source, the Beverly Hills 90210 actor is just pleased that the musician is making his ex-wife happy. “Brian knows that when Megan is happy, their children benefit,” the insider revealed. “So far, it’s one big, happy, blended family.” Austin has recently had a child with Dancing With The Star professional Sharna Burgess.

Fox and Green split in 2020, confirming that they would still work together for the sake of their young family. "As far as a family, what we've built is really cool and it's really special," he explained on an episode of his podcast. "So we decided let's make sure that we don't lose that. Let's make sure that we're always friends with each other and we're a united front with the kids. We'll do family vacations and holidays with the family and really make that a focus with the kids."

The couple have also spoken about adding to their expanding family. An insider explained last year that the couple were hoping to have children, once they were married. It is unknown if the couple are still engaged or still intend to have children together.
