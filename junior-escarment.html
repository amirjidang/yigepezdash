While growing up, Junior was fascinated by metaphysics. He would question the meaning of reality. He will ask, what is reality? Is reality an illusion? Is the argument from Darwin’s theory of evolution that our perception accurately reflects reality?

What is the meaning of truth? What is the meaning of right and wrong? Does what seems right to one man seem wrong to another based on his perception of reality? Is reality universally connected? If so, what do people choose to believe or accept as right or wrong? Junior will ask questions that no one can answer.

Junior grew up as an epistemophiliac and philomath. His favorite subjects included, but were not limited to, philosophy, American government, and Greek mythology.

He graduated from North Miami Beach Senior High School in North Miami Beach, FL, before earning his associate degree in paralegal studies in 2016 at Miami-Dade College.

He then graduated from Florida International University in 2019, where he majored in public administration and graduated with a bachelor’s degree in public administration.

He later graduated from the Florida State University College of Law with a Juris Master in 2020. Junior also earned a career certificate on American Government: Constitutional Foundations through Harvard University as well as a career certificate on America’s Written Constitution through Yale University.
