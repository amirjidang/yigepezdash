GCW Josh Barnetts Bloodsport 5 Results (2/20/21): Jon Moxley Returns

Game Changer Wrestling’s Josh Barnett’s Bloodsport 5 takes place tonight at 7 p.m. ET live on internet pay-per-view. The card is headlined by the return of Jon Moxley, who will take on Davey Boy Smith Jr. in the biggest Bloodsport match to date. WrestleZone will have live GCW Josh Barnett’s Bloodsport 5 results as the card happens.

Check out the full GCW Josh Barnett’s Bloodsport 5 card below:

  • Jon Moxley vs. Davey Boy Smith Jr.
  • Jeff Cobb vs. Tom Lawlor
  • JR Kratos vs. Chris Dickinson
  • Rocky Romero vs. Simon Grimm
  • Alex Coughlin vs. Royce Isaacs
  • Kal Jack vs. Superbeast
  • Calvin Tankman vs. Nolan Edward
  • Bad Dude Tito vs. Calder McColl

GCW Josh Barnett’s Bloodsport 5 results:

  • Calder McColl def. Bad Dude Tito via submission
  • Calvin Tankman def. Nolan Edward via TKO
  • Kal Jack def. Superbeast via submission
  • Royce Isaacs def. Alex Coughlin via submission
  • Rocky Romero def. Simon Grimm via submission
  • Chris Dickinson def. JR Kratos via submission
  • Jeff Cobb def. Tom Lawlor via submission
  • Jon Moxley def. Davey Boy Smith Jr. via KO
