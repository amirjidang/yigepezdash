Alisha Weir As Matilda Wormwood | Height, Parents, Instagram, Siblings

Alisha Weir is a Irish television and film actress who rose to fame after being cast as Laura on the series Darklands and also stars as Matilda Wormwood in Netflix’s 2022 film Roald Dahl’s Matilda the Musical.

It all began for her in 2018, when she took the stage of her drama school’s 10th Anniversary Show hosted by Talented Kids & MVW Talent Agency. It was here that she made a lasting impression and shortly thereafter appeared on the big screen in Don’t Leave Home – an event which marked the start of what would be an illustrious career as a celebrated actress.

Alisha Weir As Matilda Wormwood

Alisha Weir casts as Matilda Wormwood in the film, ‘Matilda the Musical’, which is the title character of the bestselling 1988 children’s novel Matilda by Roald Dahl.

Other casts include in the movies, Lashana Lynch as Miss Jennifer Honey, Emma Thompson as Miss Agatha Trunchbull, Stephen Graham as Mr Harry Wormwood, Andrea Riseborough as Mrs Wormwood and many more.

She also wrote on her Instagram, “I had a great time at the Irish screening for Roald Dahls Matilda The Musical at movies@dundrum. AMAZING to see everyone and @maureen.v.ward thank you. Only at Cinemas from 25th November ?❤️”.
