About

American Actress Suzanne Pleshette was born on 31st January, 1937 in Brooklyn, New York and passed away on 19th Jan 2008 Los Angeles, California, USA aged 70. She is most remembered for The Birds and The Bob Newhart Show. Her zodiac sign is Aquarius.
