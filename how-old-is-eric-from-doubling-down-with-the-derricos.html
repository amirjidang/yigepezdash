How Old Is Eric From Doubling Down With The Derricos?

Eric from Doubling Down with The Derricos is 32 years old as of 2023. He celebrates his birthday on October 19 and was born and raised in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.

Eric has been celebrating his birthday with the family since 2020. Every one of Derrico’s members never fails to surprise him with the best they can.

As he grew up in foster care and since then he learned about the value of family. Growing up without a parent was difficult for him.

However, he has now found a loving and caring family for himself. Being a part of the Derrico family has helped him in many ways.

During the Q&A session, one of his fans asked him about meeting with the Derricos and how he built a relationship with them.

Eric replied by saying Timing and Fate, he was looking for a place to rent in LA but was having a hard time getting the place.

So, he decided to go with Derrico’s rental property, which was a little expensive but was in a safe community with an organized structure.

And after getting there, he bonded with the children naturally and later with the heads of the family, and his journey with them started.

The father of the family, Deon considers Eric as his own son even though he is not related by blood and has kept under his guidance.

Eric has described himself as an honorary Derrico and has made his appearance various times on the show with good screentime.

For the Derricos family, he means more than just an honorary Dericos, and they call him a primary family member as per The Sun.

He is there with the kids and family most of the time, looking after them and helping Deon with his real estate business.
