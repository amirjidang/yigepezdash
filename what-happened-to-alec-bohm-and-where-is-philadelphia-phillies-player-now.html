What Happened To Alec Bohm?

Alec Bohm. Philadelphia’s third baseman left the Phillies’ game with a dislocated left ring finger Monday night on 11th July 2022. He suffered an injury after sliding headfirst into second base.

He exited the Phillies’ game against the host St. Louis Cardinals with a trainer holding his hand. TV cameras showed him telling Paco Figueroa, first base coach, ‘It’s broken.’

The baseman singled to left-center against St. Louis in the second inning with two outs & tried to stretch his hit into a double. He reportedly dived into second and immediately began to favor his left hand.
