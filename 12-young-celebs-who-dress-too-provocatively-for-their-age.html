Sometimes, younger people want to feel, act and be treated like an older person. It’s common for some older people to want to look and feel younger as well. They want to feel that same excitement and thrill they did in their youth. Celebrities are no different; some older celebs have been criticized for not dressing their age. At the same time, some younger celebs can hear the same thing. It’s difficult sometimes for the public to watch an innocent, young pop sensation transform into a risqué and provocative sex symbol right in front of their eyes. Many young celebrities will go through an evolution through their career. For example, Christina Aguilera began her career with a teen idol bubblegum image. She then caused a massive amount of controversy when she began to dress sexier and more provocatively. She has now evolved into a more mature image (which some say resembles movie stars like Marilyn Monroe).

For some it might just be a journey they’re on and for others it’s an expression of freedom. Perhaps, they feel that they are proud of their bodies and want to flaunt it. However, the media and the Internet generally explode into a debate about whether it’s appropriate or not. Many of the celebrities on this list are very talented, beautiful and are full of charisma. However, some may feel that they dress too sexy and provocatively for their young age. Here is a look at 10 young celebs who dress too provocatively for their age.

10) Miley Cyrus

Miley Cyrus is a singer and actress. She is the daughter of country singer Billy Ray Cyrus. She began her career with an innocent, wholesome teen bubble gum image. She had her big breakthrough when she was cast in the lead role in the Disney series, Hannah Montana. She transitioned into a very successful music career. She is best known for the hit singles, “Party In The U.S.A.”, “ We Can’t Stop” and “Wrecking Ball”. She is 22 years old. Her image began to change and became sexier, more mature and very proactive. In 2008, she made headlines at the Teen Choice Awards when she danced around a stripper pole. She has recently caused controversy with her very risque and even more provocative image. She has appeared in FHM and Maxim’s Hot 100 List. Some have criticized her for dressing too sexy, provocatively and inappropriately. Regardless, she is considered one of the hottest and top celebrities in Hollywood.

9) Demi Lovato

Demi Lovato is an actress and singer. She had her first big breakthrough in her mid teens when she was cast in the Disney TV movie, Camp Rock. She then starred in the Disney series, Sonny With A Chance. She also has had a very successful music career and has released several albums, such as Don’t Forget, Unbroken and Demi. She is currently 22 years old and certainty not a teenager anymore. However, some could argue that she still dresses too provocatively for her age. She has showed up to several Hollywood events in sexy and revealing outfits, sometimes highlighting her cleavage a little too much. However, she’s been dressing very sexy and provocatively since her teenage years. When she was 19, she turned heads showing up at the Latin Grammy’s in a dress with an insane amount of cleavage. However, she is considered to be one of the hottest and most talented new pop sensations.

8) Selena Gomez

Selena Gomez is a singer and actress. She had her big breakthrough with her role on the Disney Channel series, Wizards Of Waverley Palace. She also broke into the music industry with her band Selena Gomez and The Scene. They released the albums, Kiss & Tell, A Year Without Rain and When The Sun Goes Down. She also released a solo album, Star Dance, in 2013. She is currently 22 years old. She is sometimes criticized for dressing too sexy or provocatively. Initially, she had a sweet and innocent image but began to pursue a more sexy and provocative image. She appeared in the controversial film, Spring Breakers. The film itself was risque and depicted the girls in a highly sexual manner. However, it helped transition her into more of a mature image. Although she is in her early 20’s now, she began dressing sexier and more risque in her late teens.

7) Taylor Momsen

Taylor Momsen has been acting since she was a child. She played the role of Cindy Lou Who, in the film, How The Grinch Stole Christmas. She is best known for her role as Jenny Humphrey on the hit TV series, Gossip Girl. Later, her role with the series decreased and she began to focus on her music career. She is the lead vocalist for the band The Pretty Reckless. She is 21 years old. In her late teens, she began to dress sexier and more provocatively. Some may feel she dresses inappropriately but despite that, she is considered one of the hottest and most beautiful young celebrities.

6) Gigi Hadid

Gigi Hadid is a model and TV personality. She has been modeling since she was a child (on and off). She began modeling on a more full time basis in her later teen years. She was named one of 12 rookies of the year in the 2014 Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Edition. She is 20 years old. She has appeared in several magazines such as, Vogue, Elle and Sports Illustrated. At times, she has been criticized for dressing too sexy and provocatively. She has been seen wearing very risque and highly sexual outfits. Jaws dropped and tongues rolled at the 2015 Oscars after party. Gigi was photographed wearing a strategically black cut dress that left very little to the imagination.

5) Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande is quickly becoming one of the hottest and most talked about new pop sensations. She is a singer and actress who first rose to fame for her roles on the Disney series Victorious, and its spin-off, Sam and Cat. She will be appearing in the upcoming comedy-horror series, Scream Queens, along side Jamie Lee Curtis, Emma Roberts and Lea Michele. She has had a very successful music career. She has released the albums, Yours Truly and My Everything. She has been praised for her voice and has even been compared to Mariah Carey. She is 22 years old and has received some criticism for her sexy and provocative image. She is known for wearing white knee high boots with short skirts and crop tops. She’s even been criticized by legendary celebs like Bette Midler. Bette criticized her for her sexy, provocative and risque image. Regardless, Ariana Grande is widely considered as one of the hottest and most talented new pop stars.

4) Hailey Baldwin

Hailey Baldwin is a model and member of the Baldwin Family. She is the daughter of Stephen Baldwin. She has done some modeling and appeared in Tatler Magazine and Love Magazine. She is currently 18 years old. She is known for posting racy pictures of herself in sexy and revealing outfits on social media. She is good friends with Kendall Jenner, who everybody knows. Some argue that she dresses too sexy and provocatively. She likes to wear low tops and show off cleavage. She has appeared in magazine shoots wearing clothes that make her look 10 years older. However, she is widely regarded as one of the most beautiful new faces in modeling.

3) Kendall Jenner

Kendall Jenner is a model, socialite and reality TV personality. She is best known for her appearances on the hit reality TV series, Keeping Up With The Kardashians. She is the older sister of Kylie Jenner and the younger half-sister to Kim, Kourtney and Khloe Kardashian. She is currently 19 years old. She is known for her risque, sexy and provocative image. Since the age of 14 she has been wearing sexy and revealing outfits. She began modeling around the age of 14 and since then, has appeared on the covers of Harper’s Bazaar, Marie Claire and GQ. At the age of 18, she hosted the Much Music Video Awards in Toronto, with her sister Kylie. She was criticized by some for the revealing and sexy dress that left very little too the imagination. Some feel that she dresses too sexy and comes across older than she actually is. She is widely regarded as the hottest new face in modeling.

2) Willow Smith

Willow Smith is the daughter of actors Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith. Willow is a singer and actress. She is 14 years old, and is already known for her unique sense of fashion and style. She broke into the music business and has released the singles, “Whip My Hair, “21st Century Girl” and “Summer Fling”. However, some feel she dresses too mature and provocative for her age. Some critics have pointed out that she sometimes seems much older than she is because of the way she dresses. She received a lot of criticism for the video and song, “Summer Fling” which she did at the age of 12. Many felt that the tone and lyrics were very mature. Some critics felt that “fling” implied her having a sexual relationship, but she denied that as the meaning. She claimed it’s about the summer being too short.

1) Kylie Jenner

Kylie Jenner is the younger sister of Kendall Jenner and younger half-sister of Kim, Kourtney and Khloe Kardashian. She is best known for her appearances on the hit reality TV series, Keeping Up With The Kardashians. Time Magazine listed Kylie and sister Kendall on their 25 Most Influential Teens List. She is currently 17 years old but will soon be turning 18 in August 2015. The young teen is known for her sexy and cutting edge fashion sense. Although many may consider her beautiful, others feel she dresses too provocative, sexy and mature for her age. She has been seen at events wearing very short and revealing dresses. She also often looks much older than she actually is and could sometimes be confused with someone in their late 20's or early 30’s. She’s been dressing very sexy and provocatively since around 14 years old.
