Emma Stone Loves Wearing Red Shoes While In NYC: Style Photos

When it comes to shoes, Emma Stone has a heel up!

The Amazing Spider-Man actress, 25, often pairs her structured dresses, couture gowns, and edgy pantsuits with fiery red pumps. 

Don’t believe Us? Over the last month the fashion hit the town in three red-hot pairs that gave each of her sweet looks a dash of sophistication.

Take notes from the queen of red pumps on how to style the trend best.

Avoid the Schoolgirl Look With Red Pumps

Staying true to her flirty style, Stone showed of her slim stems in a cream and black plaid skirt with pleats, paired with a black sweater on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon on Tuesday, Oct 14. An otherwise normal schoolgirl outfit – was heroically saved by the Kurt Geiger Britton heel, which sits on a metal 4-inch cigarette heel.

Crimson Pumps Make a Sweet Ensemble Sexy

For the 92nd Street Y Film Series on Oct. 13, Stone gave her casual blue Michael Kors look featuring a white Peter Pan collar, a dose of sex appeal with red suede Aquazaura pumps, which featured a tie-up crisscross design.

Bright Red Boots Give Street Style Some Life

Here, Stone proves that there is no need to resort to black or brown for fall boot color options. Indeed, the actress stepped out in a bright red pair of Chelsea boots by Topshop on Sept. 17 in New York City. She rounded out her perfect autumn attire with cool shades, a long taupe scarf, and light-blue denim.
