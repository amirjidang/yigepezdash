Emma Stone Strips Down On The Cover of W Magazine

Emma Stone could get an easy A with her sexy new look on the cover of W Magazine.

The February issue shows the 24-year-old actress pairing blonde sex hair with a black bra and leather jacket, a far more edgier look than the clean-cut image Stone has projected since her early role in Superbad.

“Those undergarments were pretty demanding. It’s time-consuming to put on a bustier and a little corset every day,” she tells W Magazine about the barely-there outfits she would wear for her latest flick Gangster Squad. “But you’re immediately more poised than you would be in modern-day clothes. And it makes it easy to get into character.”

But don’t let her sex appeal fool you; she’s more prone to be the silly girl but is excited about taking on more serious projects. “In real life, sometimes it’s uncomfortable for me not to go for the joke,” she says. “I’ve been looking at that in myself lately. Often, joking for me is a way of diffusing the awkwardness of a situation, so it’s kind of exhilarating to be a part of projects where there’s nothing funny or lighthearted.”
