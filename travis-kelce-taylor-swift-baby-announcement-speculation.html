Swifties may need to "calm down" with hopes of a Taylor Swift pregnancy, because the "Cruel Summer" singer's appearance at the 81st Golden Globes on January 7, 2024 gained attention and not entirely in the nicest way. In addition to the endless discussion of what Selena Gomez really gossiped about to Taylor Swift and Swift's reaction to the bad joke about her relationship, Taylor Swift's body also generated buzz.

Fans Put Taylor Swift On Baby Watch For 2024

Even before Taylor Swift walked the red carpet at the Golden Globes, the topic of a Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce baby was already making its rounds in the rumor mill. PR expert, Sally Morgan, also referred to as "Psychic Sally" due to her keen ability to forecast celebrity news, made the bold statement, to Closer Magazine, that she believes Swift and Kelce will not only get engaged in 2024, but that they may also make a baby announcement.

"I think she'll marry Travis this year. They're so in love, they have a healthy relationship and there's endless trust both ways. As soon as they get married, we'll hear about a pregnancy, possibly before the year is out."

For most of Taylor Swift's career, the singer had pretty much avoided any pregnancy speculation, but that changed when she started dating Travis Kelce. This may be due to the "Bad Blood" singer being in her mid-30s or that overzealous fans are just really happy for Swift and her happiness with Kelce, however, Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have yet to address the topic as a couple. Taylor Swift has spoken about the possibilty of motherhood previously, once in 2019 and again when she was 24.

"It's impossible not to picture certain scenarios and how you would try to convince them that they have a normal life when, inevitably, there will be strange men pointing giant cameras at them from the time they are babies," Swift shared with InStyle in 2014, stating that she didn't know if she'd ever have kids.

Taylor Swift And Travis Kelce's Relationship Progressed Quickly

Discussion on Taylor Swift's love life is nothing new, but there is definitely something different about her relationship with Travis Kelce, with those closest to the couple even acknowledging the seriousness to their connection.

An insider told Us Weekly, in December 2023 that, "When they can’t physically be together, they are constantly texting, calling, and FaceTiming. Taylor’s relationship with Travis is like nothing she’s ever experienced before. They got serious fairly quickly and she was proud to show off their relationship from the jump."

Given the timeline of Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's relationship, it's true that things are progressing more seriously than her previous relationships.

Travis and Taylor were first publicly linked when the tight-end football player attended Swift's Eras Tour performance at Kansas City's Arrowhead stadium in July 2023, but talk really started brewing when Taylor Swift returned the favor and cheered Kelce on during a Cheifs game in September 2023.

"Travis very adorably put me on blast on his podcast, which I thought was metal as hell," Taylor revealed in her "Person of the Year" interview with TIME, talking about the start of their relationship, which was actually after he attended her concert. "We started hanging out right after that. So we actually had a significant amount of time that no one knew, which I'm grateful for, because we got to get to know each other."

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce engagement rumors have been circling since December 2023, when fans were certain Kelce asked Taylor Swift's dad for permission to marry the "Cruel Summer" singer. The discussion of Travis Kelce's proposal to Taylor Swift has even gone as far as people predicting it will happen on their one-year anniversary in July.

"Taylor and Travis discussed it and there is a plan," the insider claimed, according to Page Six, explaining that Kelce has held off on proposing because "they don’t want it to seem like it’s rushed insanity."

With all the theorizing on engagement rumors and pregnancy speculation, one thing can be certain for Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's relationship, and that is that they appear to be a strong, healthy and happy couple going in to 2024.
