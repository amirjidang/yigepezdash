Skip Marley

Skip Marley is a popular Jamaican musical artist as well as a music composer. He is well known as a singer as well as a songwriter actually. He has written and published various different songs until today. Some of them include Slow Down, Lions, That’s Not True, etc. The music videos of his songs are very easily available on YouTube. Apart from just YouTube, his songs can be heard from various musical applications like Deezer, Spotify, SoundCloud, TuneIn, etc.
