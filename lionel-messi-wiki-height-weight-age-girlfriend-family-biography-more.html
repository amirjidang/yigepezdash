At the age of 4, Messi joined a local club Grandoli and started playing with his elder brothers. At Grandoli, his father gave him the training. Messi was the third child of his parents. His dream of becoming a professional footballer was threatened when he was diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency. He didn’t give up playing, his treatment continued along with his training. He joined the Royal Spanish Football Federation and started playing for Barcelona.

He made his club debut on 16th October 2004, for FC Barcelona against Espanyol and international debut on 17th August 2005, for Argentine against Hungary. After winning against Espanyol, he signed a contract with Barcelona as a team player. He became one of the best players in the world at the age of 19.

The turning point for his career was in 2007, where he scored a hat-trick vs Real Madrid and ended the match with a draw while playing for Barcelona. In 2008, he was honored with the “FIFA World Player” of the year award. He is considered to be the top-goalscorer of Argentina.

As he is also involved in many charitable activities, he was appointed as UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador in 2010.

He got married to his longtime girlfriend Antonella Roccuzzo on June 30, 2017. He has three sons Thiago Messi, Matio and Ciro. His wife is a model and a dietician.
