Ben Stiller And Adam Sandler Might Be Hollywood's Most Surprising Doppelgangers

Why People Think Ben Stiller And Adam Sandler Look Alike

Ben Stiller, known for his quirky and offbeat humor, first gained attention with his work on The Ben Stiller Show in the early 1990s. He went on to star in a series of successful comedies, including There's Something About Mary, Zoolander, and Night at the Museum.

Adam Sandler, on the other hand, made a name for himself with his raunchy and irreverent comedy, starting with his stint on Saturday Night Live in the early 1990s. He's since starred in a number of blockbuster comedies, such as Billy Madison, Happy Gilmore, and The Waterboy.

Ben Stiller Says He's Often Mistaken For Adam Sandler

Stiller has acknowledged the comparison in interviews, saying that he’s always getting mistaken for Sandler. He revealed, “We also always get mistaken for each other all the time. So, just like, in life, it’s always like, ‘Yo, Sandler!’ So we’re like sending each other videos of people like going like, ‘Hey, Adam, I love you. Thank you!’”

When asked if they ever correct people, Sandler said, “Depends who’s with me. If I’m being humiliated in front of new people that are bragging about how popular I am and someone goes, ‘Oh, Mr. Stiller!’ I’m just like, ‘Uh. It’s Adam Sandler.’”

Adding to the comment, Stiller also shared, “Sometimes it’s an out. Like ‘I know who you are! Adam Sandler!’ And I’m like, ‘Nope. I’m not. I’m not lying, I swear.’ Then sometimes they’ll get mad like, ‘No, Ben Stiller! Whatever! You know what I meant!’”
