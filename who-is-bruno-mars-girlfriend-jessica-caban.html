Who Is Bruno Mars' Girlfriend Jessica Caban, And What Does She Do?

Outsiders might imagine that being Bruno Mars' girlfriend means lounging all day and spending the illustrious singer's millions. But Bruno's leading lady, Jessica Caban, has her own cash to flaunt -- and she has throughout her man's meteoric rise to fame.

And if fans need any evidence of Jessica's self-sufficiency, all they need to do is visit her Wikipedia page. Yes, the model and actress has her own entry, and she's more than just Bruno Mars' girlfriend.

The 38-year-old New Yorker does live with Bruno, reports The Knot, and has since 2012. But the adorable couple has been together since 2011 (the first year of their relationship was long-distance). And despite the fact that he hardly ever talks about Jessica publicly, Bruno is clearly smitten.

For one thing, Bruno doesn't often appear on Jessica's Instagram. But when he does, it's not as a superstar singer -- it's just as her loving boyfriend. Most often, Caban posts pics with Bruno that also feature her family. But she also frequently gets together with Mars and his family, including his five siblings.

It's downright adorable, actually. But get this: the pair even shares a pup, a Rottweiler named Geronimo. That's not to say Jessica's totally oblivious to her man's fame, though, even if their life together is the picture of domestic and familial bliss.

Clearly, Caban reps for Mars, but that's not all she's about. She keeps busy with her fashion collaborations, which started all the way back in 2002 with a modeling gig for JLO, Jennifer Lopez's fashion line.

Caban also has her own swimwear line, aptly named J Marie Swim, and fans won't find a single pic of Bruno on that promotional page.

Jessica clearly likes to keep business and pleasure separate -- and maybe that's the key to her long-term relationship with one of the planet's biggest musical superstars?

Of course, modeling brands like Versace and Gucci on her IG also puts Jessica squarely in the spotlight -- but only to her 271K followers. That's no small number to scoff at, but it is interesting that Bruno has 22.6 million followers, and yet his girlfriend is able to fly somewhat under the radar with her own interests and business endeavors.

Both halves of this famous couple clearly stay busy, though. Besides modeling and her business ventures, Jessica's also been spotted on shows like 'Jane the Virgin' -- she played a character named Sonia for four episodes (and Bruno appeared as well, but to sing, of course).

And while it might seem strange that Bruno Mars' girlfriend is living a pretty normal life, fans are still totally jealous of the gorgeous model!
