Ty Gibbs Girlfriend: Is Ty Gibbs In A Relationship?

Tyler Randal Gibbs was born on October 4, 2002, is an American professional stock car racing driver. He competes full-time in the NASCAR Xfinity Series, driving the No. 54 Toyota Supra for Joe Gibbs Racing. Gibbs was the 2021 ARCA Menards Series champion.

The grandson of Pro Football Hall of Fame coach and NASCAR Hall of Fame team owner Joe Gibbs, Ty Gibbs made a statement in the 2020 ARCA Menards Series season, earning a series-leading six wins in just 16 starts. Gibbs’ solid performance led the way for his team, owned by father and former driver Coy Gibbs, to win the 2020 ARCA Menards Series owner’s championship.

Gibbs won the ARCA Menards Series championship in 2021 while racing a partial schedule in the NASCAR Xfinity Series. In his first career national series start, Gibbs won the NASCAR Xfinity Series race on the Daytona International Speedway Road Course in February 2021. He also added wins at Charlotte, Watkins Glen, and Kansas in the 2021 Xfinity Series season.
