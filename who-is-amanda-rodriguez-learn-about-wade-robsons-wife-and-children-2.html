Wade Robson is an established Australian dancer and choreographer, married to his gorgeous wife, Amanda Rodriguez.

The dancer received the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Choreography for the dance number “Ramalama (Bang Bang)” in So You Think You Can Dance. Later, he joined the Fox television dance series So You Think You Can Dance as a guest judge and choreographer in 2007.

Wade has directed music videos and world tours for pop acts such as NSYNC, and Britney Spears began performing as a dancer when he was five.

The dancer made several headlines after he reported the accusation case against the legendary pop artist Micahel Jackson.

During Jackson’s trial in 2005, Robson testified under oath that Jackson had never misbehaved. Surprisingly, he reversed his position and filed a late creditor claim and a civil lawsuit against the Jackson estate, alleging that Jackson had abused him for eight years, from 1990 to 1997. Robson dated Michael Jackson’s niece Brandi, the daughter of Jackie Jackson, for seven years. Robson asked Jackson to set the two up. The two were friends for ten years before the beginning of their relationship.

As per the source, Justin wrote “Cry Me a River” about Spears cheating on him with Robson. Neither Spears nor Timberlake has commented on these rumors. Robson also dated dancer Mayte Garcia from 2000 until 2001.
