Zach Ertz Father

Zach’s father, Doug Ertz, was also a standout on the gridiron, playing college football at Lehigh University from 1981-84 before moving to California for work, shortly after graduation. Although his football career didn’t take off – he’s now the vice president of worldwide sales at Wind River, software and company – Doug has been one of his son’s biggest supporters.

“First of all, there’s a sense of relief. I know Zach was going through a stressful time. I think, in Zach’s mindset, he believes he was the best tight end in the draft. I believe time will tell. He thought he would be the first tight end to go, but he didn’t. And I think when the ‘Niners had the chance to pick him but they traded up, that created some gyrations for him.

There were a lot of things going on that first day, and I know he was really frustrated,” Doug told The Morning Call, just after Zach was drafted in 2013. Zach’s first athletic love was basketball, but it didn’t take long for him to find a place on the football field and he frequently credits his parents for sparking that interest.
