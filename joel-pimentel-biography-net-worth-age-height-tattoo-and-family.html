Joel Pimentel Biography; Net Worth, Age, Height, Tattoo And Family

Joel Pimentel is an American musician best known as a member of CNCO, the famous Latin American boys band in Miami. The band was formed in 2015 and has helped in shaping the music career of Joel Pimentel. He grew up in California with his mother and three brothers. His love for music started at the tender age of 8 when he began taking music lessons.

In 2015, Joel competed in the TV reality show “La Banda”  and was part of the finalists. He was selected to be part of the five-boy band called CNCO. The band performed Spanish songs and was a delight to watch. The band signed a five-year contract with ‘Sony Music Latin,’ which helped in showcasing new talent in Latin music.
