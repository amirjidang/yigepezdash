Heist Netflix: Who Is Heather Tallchief And Where Is She Now? Everything To Know

Heather Tallchief is not on Wikipedia despite being a very famous personality who successfully got away from the FBI for over a decade. 

On October 1, 1993, a 21-year-old girl successfully took $3 million from an armored vehicle company and led her way to the biggest heist in the history of Las Vegas.

Surprisingly, the American police and even the FBI could not spot find the girl even until the time she fled away from the States.

After about 12 years of the incident, a woman named Heather Tallchief came to the State Marshal’s office with a lawyer committing her crime; she was indeed the 21-year-old girl.

After that, she served 5 years in prison and paid $2.9 million that led her to live an everyday life again in the states.

After about 11 years of her release from prison, a Netflix Documentary is being made named The Heist, and it is about her life story.

Heather Tallchief Age And Wikipedia

Heather Tallchief’s current age is 49 years old.

However, there is no actual evidence regarding her exact date of birth.

Her age is extracted on the basis that she was 21 years old in the year 1993.

She was a young health worker when she was fed up with living life by treating patients with cancer ad AIDS.

She wanted to do something different when she met a then 48-year-old man, a murderer named Roberto Solis, who served 20 years in prison.

He dragged her to the world of money, drugs, and sex, which changed everything she was.

They, in a planned way, robbed an armored vehicle company and a way to Amsterdam with various changes in the middle way to get away from police.

She was separated from Roberto and was living a life as a British citizen in the UK. Later, Heather decided to return to the United States and accept her fault.

Who Is Heather Tallchief Son?

Heather Tallchief has a 26-year-old son Dylan, who is very close to her and is the reason for her submission to the States police.

Just after Heather and Roberto left for Amsterdam, about 2 years later, she had a son.

After giving birth, she realized that her son has to grow up in a better environment, so she left Roberto.

Then, she had another partner with who she raised her son.

When Dylan turned 10, Heather decided that her son will be an American and live a normal life. So, she came to the States for him.

Then things happened with time, and Dylan graduated in 2019, with his mother by his side, and out of prison.

Where Is Heather Tallchief Now?

Heather Tallchief is not in the United States and is continuing her medical work like she used to do.

Apart from that, she collaborates with Netflix and is making a movie based on her life story.

She has become an average person now since she completed her sentence more than a decade ago.
