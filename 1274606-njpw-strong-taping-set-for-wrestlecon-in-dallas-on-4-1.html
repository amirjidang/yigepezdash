NJPW STRONG Taping Set For WrestleCon In Dallas On 4/1

NJPW is coming to WrestleCon 2022.

New Japan Pro Wrestling has announced that the company will be holding a NJPW STRONG taping at WrestleCon 2022 in Dallas, Texas.

From NJPW:

After NJPW STRONG’s Autumn Attack saw action come to DFW Texas this past September, STRONG heads back to Dallas on April 1 2022!

WrestleCon will this year see a special STRONG taping at 5PM on April 1, and meet and greets are also planned!

More information is coming soon!

Officially official! pic.twitter.com/sZpncPyseX

Wrestling stars such as Bray Wyatt, Matt Cardona, Killer Kross and Scarlett Bordeaux have all been confirmed for this year’s WrestleCon event.
