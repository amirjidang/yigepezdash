Heidi Klum's Best Halloween Costumes Through the Years

Heidi Klum is the queen of Halloween. 

Through the years, Klum has gone above and beyond when it comes to dressing up at her annual spooky soirée. 

One of her most memorable looks came at her 2022 bash, which saw her disguised as a giant worm. The getup featured creepy textured — and slimy —  skin complete with ridges and a curved silhouette. Klum revealed ahead of the party that the costume took over 14 hours to create.

Klum’s husband, Tom Kaulitz, who went as a fisherman, opened up about his wife’s ensemble, telling Us Weekly at the time, “She just came up with the worm. She’s like that. She gets up in the morning and she’s like, ‘I’m gonna be a worm,’ and I’m like, OK. … I’m running out of options [for my costume].’”
