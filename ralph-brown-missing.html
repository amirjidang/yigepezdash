Ralph Brown Missing Whats The Mystery Behind His Disappearance?

The strange disappearance of Ralph Brown and the car he was last seen driving has sent shockwaves across the nation. Social media platforms have been abuzz with news about Brown getting lost, and everyone is praying for his safe return. 

According to KOIN-TV, Brown had spent four hours traveling near Cornelius in circles around a 30-mile radius before going missing.

The Washington County Sheriff’s Office then eventually discovered the vehicle 40 feet below Willamette River at Newberg on Friday, but it contained the human remains of an unidentified person.

We can only hope that the news regarding his whereabouts will soon provide closure to loved ones.

It was truly a bittersweet moment when the team of volunteer divers finally discovered Brown’s car at Adventures With Purpose.

This weekend, they found Mr. Brown’s vehicle after searching the region ten times in the previous year. However, it remains unclear just what became of him following his disorientation on that fateful night. 

Understandably, his family is eager to put this cold case to rest and has been relying on independent sleuthing YouTubers such as Adventures With Purpose, whose mission is to solve any unsolved mysteries while missing person disappearances through relentless searches and investigations. 

Their YouTube channel, with over 2.79 million subscribers, brings an army of people who are dedicated to helping these families get closure.

Since 2019, the group Texas EquuSearch has experienced tremendous success in solving cold cases. In March 2020, the team discovered the body of a Pennsylvania man who had been missing for over 16 years. 

Then, several months later, in January 2021, members of the organization located three vehicles in the Brazos River, one of which belonged to a woman that had been missing since 2016. 

The courageous divers have not only provided much-needed answers to families. Still, they have given another example: what once seemed impossible can be attained through sheer dedication and hard work.

Additionally, their newfound knowledge and experiences will remain a beacon of hope to any individual who ever encounters a missing person case in their lives.

For the Brown family, Ralph’s disappearance has forced them to ask themselves some difficult and heartbreaking questions about his whereabouts. His supposedly “devastated” children only wish for him to come back to them safe and sound.

It remains unclear why he disappeared in the first place; people have speculated it was due to his financial struggles but that doesn’t ease any of their worries or pain during this trying time.

Law enforcement has recently taken up his case once again, hoping to bring closure —and possibly reunite the Brown family—by finding out more information on Ralph’s current situation. 

Everyone is hoping for a positive outcome that enables the kids to further experience their father’s presence in their lives.

Amanza Smith and Ralph Brown’s Marriage 

Despite being married for two years, Amanza Smith and Brown’s relationship ended abruptly in 2012. Since then, their children have had to adjust to life without their father’s presence and after an almost nine-year hiatus, it seems that he is still absent from their lives. 

Though the viewers of Selling Sunset got a glimpse into Almanza’s complicated past with her ex-husband Ralph Brown, there is a whole other side to this story that has yet to be told.

About Ralph Brown 

Ralph Brown had quite a journey during his NFL career, hopping from team to team for nearly a decade. He started in 2000 with the New York Giants and lasted until 2003, before moving on to the Minnesota Vikings until 2005. 

After his stint with the Vikings, he was recruited by the Cleveland Browns for the 2006 season and traveled further West in 2007 for three years with the Arizona Cardinals. 

However, Brown’s professional career wasn’t only memorable because of his successful playing days; it was also marked by an eventful marriage with ex-wife Amanza Smith that unfortunately lasted much shorter than his time in the NFL – two short years.

Despite their split up, they successfully co-parent two children together.
