Davie504 Biography And Net Worth

Davide Biale known by his online alias Davie504 is an Italian YouTuber, musician, and performer.

Davide Biale was born on April 5, 1994, in Savona in Italy.

He is best known for playing bass guitar, primarily with a slapping technique, and creating several covers and viral videos.

Davide Biale began playing bass guitar in high school after being influenced by Kiss group bassist Gene Simmons.

In 2011, he created a YouTube channel named, Davie504 and began to upload unconventional cover performances of songs in 2012. His channel has gathered up to over 9 million subscribers.

He also performs musical challenges, which are often suggested to him by comments on his videos, such as playing 0 Strings Bass Solo.

In 2017, Chowny Bass released the Davie504 signature bass guitar, and in May 2019, Biale was listed as number two on MusicRadar’s list of “the 20 hottest bassists in the world today”. He received his Youtube Gold Play Button later that year, which he turned into a custom bass alongside Amnesia Guitars.

Davie504 Net Worth

Davide Biale is financially stable. He has an estimated net worth valued at $3.6 million.
