Chilli Reportedly Unbothered By Usher's Marriage Proposal Revelations

Chilli of TLC is reportedly “unbothered” following ex-lover, Usher‘s, revelations about a failed proposal to the R&B singer. The musicians’ former relationship has been put back in the spotlight after the newly-married star shared with PEOPLE that he once popped the question and was denied.

According to the same outlet, a “source close to” Chilli said that the “Waterfalls” singer is “unbothered by the recent hoopla involving her past relationship.”

Her response stems from the recent 2024 Super Bowl half-time performer, where he recalled, “I wanted to marry her. I proposed and she told me no. I went through a great deal of pain after that, not trusting women or wanting to open up.”

Fortunately, both stars have moved on in their love life, as Usher just tied the knot to his longtime partner and Chilli is dating former Boy Meets World star, Matthew Lawrence.

“I’ve never experienced anything like this,” Chilli told PEOPLE last year. “To this day we have not had one argument.”

Additionally, a rep for the 52-year-old stated, “I’ve been with Chilli since 2005 and I’ve never seen her this in love. She is glowing. They are really cute together.”

On Valentine’s Day (Feb. 14), the publication also shared exclusive photos of the Confessions icon, newly wed.

“We can confirm that Usher and Jennifer Goicoechea took the next step in their relationship and did get married on Sunday night in Las Vegas surrounded by close friends and family,” Usher’s rep shared. “They both look forward to continuing to raise their children together surrounded by love and thank everyone for the well wishes.”

Additionally, Usher shared photos from their unforgettable night with the caption: “One of them Ones [diamond ring emoji] 2.11.24.”

The ceremony reportedly took place right after Super Bowl LVIII, sealing the deal for the parents of two.
