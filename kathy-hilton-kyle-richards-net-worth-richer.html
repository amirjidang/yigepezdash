Who Has A Higher Net Worth Kathy Hilton or Kyle Richards?

Since Kyle Richards is one of the most famous Real Housewives stars and Kathy Hilton has such a famous last name, it's no wonder that fans are fascinated by their lifestyles. Fans wanted to know Kyle's reaction to Kathy joining RHOBH for season 11, and it was sweet seeing their sisterly connection blossom throughout the episodes. In season 11 alone, fans were treated to a fabulous dinner party at Kathy's house, a Christmas meal at Kyle's, and a stay at a gorgeous hotel. Now that Kathy will return for season 12, it'll be fun to see what they get up to.

Fans are curious about what Kathy and Kyle's financial situations are like and which sister is richer. Keep reading to find out whether Kathy Hilton or Kyle Richards has a higher net worth.

Does Kathy Hilton or Kyle Richards Have A Higher Net Worth?

Kathy Hilton has a $350 million net worth and that is higher than Kyle Richards's net worth.

Kyle Richards has a $100 million net worth, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

While both sisters have been wealthy for a long time and they definitely had money before singing onto the reality series, they do seem to make good money for appearing on RHOBH.

TMZ reported that Kathy Hilton agreed to join season 12 of RHOBH and Kathy reportedly wanted to be given more money. Radar Online reported that Kyle is paid $500,000 for each season. Camille Grammer even said that Kyle made more than anyone back in the first season: according to Camille, the cast was given $36,000 for that first season and Kyle was paid $134,000.

Kyle Richards Has Done Well In The Acting Industry

Horror movie fans were able to watch Kyle Richards come back to play Lindsey Wallace in 2021's Halloween Kills. Kyle played Lindsey in the 1978 Halloween movie when she was just a kid, and in Halloween Kills, Lindsey helps Tommy Doyle (Anthony Michael Hall) and a group of fed up Haddonfield residents fight Michael Myers.

It seems that Kyle would have been paid nicely to return to the franchise.

Kyle also starred in the Peacock Christmas movie The Housewives of the North Pole alongside Betsy Brandt.

Kyle Richards was interviewed about the Christmas movie and shared that she wasn't a huge fan of the movie's title. While at first it was called The Real Housewives of the North Pole, Kyle wanted the "real" to be gone.

According to UPI, Kyle said, "I cannot escape this world. It was very much its own thing. It's a movie. It's acting and it's separate. I want to keep my acting life separate from my reality world. So I definitely fought to not have that be the title, but clearly lost."

Bravo TV reported that Kyle enjoys celebrating the winter holidays each year so she was thrilled to sign onto the project. Kyle explained, "I also was just drawn to the fact that it's just a feel-good movie. It's a movie about friendship and love and family and young love. And it is just something that makes you laugh and makes you cry and makes you watch with an unbelievable smile on your face. The script was amazing."

Kathy Hilton Has Been Busy Filming 'This Is Paris'

When Paris Hilton got married to Carter Reum in November 2021, the celebration and the preparations leading up to it were filmed for a reality series that is available to stream on Peacock. And Kathy Hilton was part of it, which was fun for fans who enjoyed watching Kathy on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills.

When Andy Cohen interviewed Kathy Hilton for his SiriusXM show "Andy Cohen Live," he said that people said that she and Kim Richards were mad that no one was allowed to use cell phones at the wedding. Kathy answered with her typical sense of humor: "What shocked me is that I even left my phone at home."

Kathy told Hollywood Life that Paris had "cold feet" beforehand.

While Kathy has made money from various projects, Kathy Hilton acted as a kid: according to Nicki Swift, she was also in the same industry as her sisters Kyle and Kim Richards. She had small parts on Happy Days, The Rockford Files, Bewitched, and Family Affair. She was even in an advertisement for Dial soap when she was just a baby.
