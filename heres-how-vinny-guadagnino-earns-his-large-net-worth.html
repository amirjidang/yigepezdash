Here's How Vinny Guadagnino Earns His Large Net Worth

Reality TV OG Vinny Guadagnino has come a long way since he starred in the reality TV show that launched his career into the world of reality TV. Vinny had just graduated with a degree in Political science from CUNY College of Staten Island and was working as a political aide for a state assemblyman when he received the call that changed the trajectory of his life.

Over the course of his career, the Jersey Shore star has widened his net to accommodate acting, co-hosting shows, influencing, and even having his talk show. Thanks to his many engagements, Vinny enjoys an impressive net worth.

8 Vinny Guadagnino Started On Jersey Shore

The reality TV star got his claim to fame on MTV's Jersey Shore. But more than just fame, featuring as a cast member on the show projected Vinny into a life of affluence. He joined the show in 2009, fresh out of college, without a job, and even considered quitting the show when they weren't getting paid in the premiere season.

"We did the first season for nothing, zero dollars, except whatever we made at the Shore Store," he relayed on Vulture's Jersey Shore: An Oral History.

Against his better judgment, Vinny decided to continue with the show. His decision to wait paid off, as he was earning a whopping $90,000 per episode by the sixth season.

7 Vinny Guadagnino Appeared On Dancing With The Stars

Vinny appeared on the 31st season of ABC's Dancing With The Stars, where he partnered with professional dancer Koko Iwasaki to contest for the Mirror Ball trophy. Per Variety, celebrities are paid $125,000 for the rehearsal period and the show's first two weeks.

After the initial payment, bonuses are paid per episode if the celebrity remains in the competition. There's a $10,000 bonus for the third and fourth week, $20,000 for the fifth and sixth, $30,000 for the seventh and eighth, and $50,000 for the final two weeks.

In the eighth week, Vinny was eliminated with Heidi D'Amelio in a shocking double elimination.

6 Vinny Guadagnino Is A Talk Show Host

In 2013, the Keto Guido began hosting his talk show, The Show with Vinny. The hybrid talk show, which aired on MTV, captured the reality TV star as he invited celebrities to converse with him and his family over homemade Italian food.

Vinny's show was quite different from the regular talk shows, as there were no live audiences except his family and friends. Although the show only aired for one season, it featured notable celebrities, including Lil Wayne, Ciara, Mark Wahlberg, and Iggy Azalea.

5 Vinny Guadagnino Has Reality TV On Lockdown

Featuring onJersey Shore opened the portal of reality shows to the Keto Guido. Since making his reality TV debut, Vinny has appeared in several shows, including The Great Food Truck Race, TheMasked Dancer, Silent Library, and Jersey Shore: Family Vacation, among others.

Of his stacked reality show profile, Vinny and his Jersey Shore co-star Pauly D play significant roles as hosts and main casts at Double Shot At Love, and Revenge Prank.

4 Vinny Guadagnino Owns a Clothing Brand

Outside of reality TV, Vinny Guadagnino is a fashion entrepreneur. In 2017, the Jersey Shore OG launched his clothing brand, which was initially called Name Brand NYC and subsequently changed to Vinny G.

The clothing brand is a collection of tee shirts with different inscriptions and designs for men, women, and children. Vinny G is Vinny's second clothing brand after IHAV, launched as a philanthropic project geared towards his anti-bullying campaign.

3 Vinny Guadagnino Is An Actor

The former political aide gave up his dreams of going to law school for acting. To pursue his interests and shape his career, he dedicated time to taking acting classes. Although the reality TV star has not broken the ground in his acting career, his devotion soon paid off with a main role in Jersey Shore Shark Attack and guest roles in 90210 and The Hard Times of RJ Berger.

2 Vinny Guadagnino Invests In Real Estate

Vinny Guadagnino likes to put his money where the house is. The reality TV star is the owner of multiple homes in New Jersey and New York. In 2021, he showed off breathtaking views of the stunning Hollywood Hills home he acquired for his 33rd birthday the previous month.

Barely months after, Vinny sold the property for $3.85 million, $350,000 more than he had purchased it for. In August 2022, Vinny bought a luxe $3.43M pad in New York.

1 How Much Is Vinny Guadagnino Worth Today?

​​​​​​​

As of this writing, Vinny has amassed a net worth of $5 million, making the reality Star OG one of the richest cast members of Jersey Shore. Thanks to appearing on several reality shows, acting, entrepreneurship, real estate, and book sales, the Jersey Shore Shark Attack star seems to be reaching for the stars.
