Known for being one of America’s most iconic and popular film directors, Lilly Wachowski has an estimated net worth of $125 million. Born in Chicago, Illinois in 1967 to a nurse and a businessman, Wachowski became involved with the performing arts from an early age at the Whitney Young High School.

After starring in several productions at the high school level, Wachowski attended Emerson College in Boston. However, Wachowski dropped out of college just before graduation to return to Chicago so he can run a carpentry company and create comic books. Best known for being the director of all three Matrix movies, Wachowski has also written and produced the movies "V for Vendetta" and "Speed Racer."

Throughout his long career in showbiz, Wachowski has been nominated for numerous awards and in 2000 was presented with a Saturn Award for Best Director for "The Matrix." In addition to his career as a film director and producer, Wachowski has also been credited for helping to write and produce The Matrix video games as well as several popular comic books.
