Is Oprah Beloved Or Overrated? Heres What These Celebs Have Said About Her

There are three levels of status a Hollywood celebrity can attain. There's the rising star, the high-profile celeb, and Oprah Winfrey. Of course, it's impossible to reach the level of Winfrey.

Oprah Winfrey is one of the most recognizable and influential celebrities in the world. Winfrey first gained fame as the host of The Oprah Winfrey Show from 1986 to 2011. Winfrey's groundbreaking show featured several memorable moments. She's larger than life and more than just a celebrity. Winfrey is one of the most beloved individuals in television history.

Of course, no celeb is without enemies. For all the celebrities that love Winfrey, there are a few that can't stand her. They even think she's overrated. Well, it balances out with all the celebs that adore her. Many of her friends are some of the most famous celebrities of all time. On the other hand, many stars feel she's very overrated. Most in the media assume everyone loves her, but that's not the case. It's time to a closer look at the Queen of all media. Is Oprah Beloved Or Overrated: Here's What These Celebs Think Of Her

15 Ellen DeGeneres - Beloved 

Ellen DeGeneres and Oprah Winfrey have a special friendship. Indeed, Winfrey set the stage for DeGeneres to take over as the Queen of Daytime TV. However, the friendship goes much deeper than that. In the 90s, DeGeneres came out during an interview with Winfrey that made major headlines. Winfrey even portrayed DeGeneres's therapist in her hit TV series Ellen.

14 50 Cent – Overrated 

In the early 2000s, 50 Cent was on the verge of becoming a true superstar. He felt appearing on The Oprah Winfrey Show would prove he reached a high level of fame. However, Oprah Winfrey criticized his lyrics and music. Indeed, she refused to have him on. 50 Cent decided to become her enemy in a last-ditch effort to gain more fame.

13 John Travolta - Beloved

John Travolta and Oprah Winfrey have been friends for decades. Indeed, Travolta appeared as a guest on her show many times. Travolta often praises Winfrey for her charity work. Winfrey and Travolta have worked together on several different humanitarian projects. Travolta gushes over Winfrey and is one of Winfrey's biggest fans in the industry.

12 Janet Jackson – Overrated 

Oprah Winfrey and the Jackson family have been friends for years. However, Winfrey and Janet Jackson recently had a falling out. Winfrey publicly supported the documentary Finding Neverland, which portrayed Michael Jackson in a negative light. Janet was upset that Winfrey supported the movie and interviewed the participants.

11 Jimmy Kimmel – Beloved

Jimmy Kimmel is best known as the host of Jimmy Kimmel Live! Kimmel first met Oprah Winfrey years ago before Kimmel had his show. Kimmel knew right away the reason everyone loves Winfrey. Indeed, Kimmel is one of her biggest fans and is up there with John Travolta. He often praises Winfrey and is proud to know her. Kimmel and Winfrey remain great friends.

10 Mo’Nique – Overrated

It's safe to assume Mo'Nique is not a fan of Oprah Winfrey. Indeed, the two have been feuding for years. Mo'Nique feels that Winfrey is not supportive and tried to make her career difficult. Mo'Nique is known for her outspoken nature and often criticizes Winfrey. Mo'Nique often calls out Winfrey for her hypocritical opinions.

9 Meryl Streep – Beloved

Meryl Streep is one of the few celebs that can compare to Oprah Winfrey's star power. Streep is an icon and legend in her own right. Streep and Winfrey are both known for speaking their minds. Indeed, they're both excellent friends and are very supportive. Streep even supports Winfrey running for President of the U.S.

8 Ludacris – Overrated

In 2006, Ludacris appeared on The Oprah Winfrey Show along with the cast of the film Crash. However, Ludacris felt blindsided by Oprah Winfrey. Winfrey criticized Ludacris for his lyrics and the content of his music. Since the interview, Ludacris openly discusses the experience and believes that Winfrey doesn't support hip-hop.

7 Mindy Kaling –Beloved

Mindy Kaling has nothing but kind words for her friend Oprah Winfrey. Winfrey and Kaling have become good friends over the years. Indeed, Winfrey sent Kaling a truck of baby gifts to Kaling's home. Kaling often praises Winfrey for her support, friendship, and mentorship. Winfrey has helped Kaling's career in many ways.

6 Kid Rock - Overrated

It's no secret that Kid Rock isn't a fan of Oprah Winfrey. However, Rock has criticized Winfrey over the years. Recently, Rock went on a profanity-laced rant about Winfrey during an event in Nashville. He ended up facing a massive backlash that cost him work and even a restaurant. It's safe to assume Rock and Winfrey won't be going out for dinner any time soon.

5 Tom Hanks – Beloved

In 2018, the media and several celebs started pushing for Oprah Winfrey to run for President. Indeed, Winfrey is known for being one of the most influential stars. Tom Hanks and Winfrey have been good friends for years. Hanks fully supports Winfrey if she ever ran for President and will vote for her without hesitation.

4 Ice Cube – Overrated

Ice Cube is critical of Oprah Winfrey and her stance on hip-hop music. Cube pointed out that Winfrey had Cederic The Entertainer and Eve on her show to promote the film Barbershop. Of course, Cube is the star of the movie, but Winfrey didn't invite him. Cube felt slighted by Winfrey and singled out. He notes she often had criminals on her show but drew the line at hip-hop stars.

3 Gayle King - Beloved 

It's a well-known fact that Oprah Winfrey and Gayle King are best friends forever. Indeed, they praise each other, go on vacations together, and just love to hangout. King gushes over Winfrey often and praises her BFF. King notes that Winfrey is a really good person that wants to help those in need.

2 Angelina Jolie – Overrated 

Oprah Winfrey and Angelina Jolie are both known for their humanitarian work around the world. They seem like they'd be good friends, but that's not the case. Indeed, Winfrey asked Jolie to participate in Winfrey's Leadership Academy For Girls In South Africa. Surprisingly, Jolie turned down the offer. Sources speculate that Jolie is still upset that Winfrey publicly took Jennifer Aniston's side when Brad Pitt left her for Jolie.

1 Reese Witherspoon - Beloved 

Reese Witherspoon considers Oprah Winfrey to be more than just a good friend or media mogul. Witherspoon worked with Winfrey on the film A Wrinkle In Time. However, Witherspoon turns to Winfrey for advice and guidance. Indeed, Witherspoon regards Winfrey has her biggest mentor. Winfrey helped Witherspoon navigate through the industry. They remain the best of friends.
