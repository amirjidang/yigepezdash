Did Love Is Blind Star SK Cheat On His Fiance Raven?

Love is Blind's unique premise has kept fans tuning in for three seasons (so far), and each season has its unique drama, twists, and turns. In the third season, Raven and SK emerged as one of the strongest couples, but post-reunion, some surprising news seemed to hint that they wouldn't last.

Rumors that SK had cheated on Raven began to swirl, but SK had a surprising retort to the accusations. Here's what Raven and SK have said about their relationship—and post-Love is Blind breakup.

Raven And SK Were Paired Together On Season 3 Of The Show

Season 3 of Love Is Blind officially premiered in 2022. The show pairs couples without them initially getting to see each other's faces. Instead, they get to know each other from behind a wall, where they can ask each other questions and build an emotional bond before judging them by their appearance.

Of course, this certainly makes for entertaining TV for many reality TV lovers, as they watch to see which couples will survive, and which will fall apart, whether before or after they say "I do."

One notable couple from the third season of the show was Raven and SK, whose relationship seemed to grow slowly and—an interesting point for a reality show—without much drama.

While the couple appeared to build a strong connection as they got to know each other, many fans were left shocked when SK said 'no' at the altar, which unsurprisingly left Raven in tears.

Fans were left reeling by the news, with some taking to Reddit with comments such as: "I just got to that part and screamed WHAT?? The way she hugged him just proved to me that they have a lovely and mature relationship. It felt like SK was saying not yet, not rejecting her."

During the reunion episode of the show, the pair confirmed that they were still together. This delighted many fans, who thought the pair suited one another reasonably well.

However, this wouldn't be the last shock to come to fans. Not long after the episode aired, rumors began to swirl around in regard to the couple's relationship.

Cheating Rumors Have Been Circulating Online

During the reunion episode, the couple stated that they were working on a long-distance relationship. They also explained that they felt that it had helped their relationship to 'grow', despite SK living in California and Raven living in Texas.

However, despite all seemingly going well between the Love Is Blind couple, fans were soon met by surprise when cheating rumors started making their rounds on the internet. The cheater in question turned out to be SK, who allegedly cheated with a woman who revealed the details on TikTok.

ATikToker accused him of being unfaithful to Raven, with her video implying that she even went on a date with the Love Is Blind contestant after the show ended.

Raven initially responded to the claims via social media, stating that 'rumors are rumors' and that things were 'good' between the pair. She also added that she was 'sticking by her man'.

In an interview with Life & Style, SK also shut down the rumors that were circulating online. He stated that a lot of people didn't realize that he and Raven had actually split up when the show came to an end, noting, "A lot of people have misconceptions about the timeline of our relationship."

He also emphasized that when he was seeing other women, he wasn't dating Raven during that time. Still, the situation seemed unexpected, given that Raven and SK didn't publicize any kind of breakup during their relationship.

Raven And SK Have Confirmed That They Are No Longer Together

Despite being a fan-favorite couple, the pair, unfortunately, didn't work out.

The couple confirmed the news via their Instagram stories, with Raven writing: "We are saddened to announce that we have decided to go our separate ways. Due to on-going legal proceedings surrounding these allegations, we can not provide additional details and ask that you please respect our privacy during this hard time. Thank you for following our love story and believing in us."

While Raven and SK didn't work out, where are the rest of the Love Is Blind season 3 couples now? There is some good news. Alexa and Brennon, another fan-favorite couple are still together, while Colleen and Matt are also still married and plan to move in together, finally, sometime soon.
