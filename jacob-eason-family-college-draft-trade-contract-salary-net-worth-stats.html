Jacob Eason Family, College, Draft, Trade, Contract, Salary, Net Worth, Stats

Jacob Eason is an American football quarterback for the Seattle Seahawks of the National Football League.

Jacob Eason Family

Jacob Eason was born to Tony and Christine Eason and was raised in Lake Stevens, Washington. His father, Tony Eason, played college football at the University of Notre Dame.

Jacob Eason College

Jacob Eason attended the University of Georgia before transferring to the University of Washington.

Jacob Eason Draft

Jacob Eason was drafted by the Indianapolis Colts with the 122nd overall pick in the fourth round of the 2020 NFL Draft.

Jacob Eason Trade

After one start in his first season with the Indianapolis Colts, Eason was waived and claimed by the Seattle Seahawks on October 20, 2021.

Jacob Eason Contract

Jacob Eason signed a 4 year, $4,027,264 contract with the Indianapolis Colts, including a $732,264 signing bonus, $732,264 guaranteed, and an average annual salary of $1,006,816.

Jacob Eason Salary
