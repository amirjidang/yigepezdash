In 1874, a Swiss timepiece maker named Georges Edouard Piaget established a workshop in a tiny village in the Jura mountains of Switzerland called La Cote aux Fees. It initially focused on the creation of pocket watches and high-precision clock movements for other prestigious timepiece companies. In 1991, his son took over and refocused the company’s focus to the manufacture of wristwatches.

The company has been able to cultivate its image of being a “watchmaker jeweler. In 1988, the luxury goods specialist from Switzerland called Richemont, then known as Vendome, bought the timepiece company.

The company has always been known for its fine craftsmanship. While it began as a manufacturer of watch movements, it eventually evolved to create and market its own line of prestigious timepieces in the 20th century. Piaget has been in the forefront of the ultra-thin movement, including the thinnest tourbillon movement in the world at 3.5 millimeters. The company has also maintained its tradition of hand-painted miniatures and hand set jewelries. It only uses diamonds that are of the highest color and clarity.

It is one of the most prestigious jewelry brand in the world. Here now is a list of the top 10 most expensive Piaget watches.

 

10. Piaget Dancer Large White Gold Pave Diamond Watch - $87,000

The Piaget Dancer Large White Gold Pave Diamond Watch has a diamond dial and a bracelet made of white gold that weighs 18 karats. The bracelet is also lined with diamonds and has a hidden folding clasp. This large watch has manual winding movement and measures 38 millimeters in diameter and 4.2 millimeters in thickness. It has ultra thin movement and bezel made of diamonds.

 

9. Piaget Limelight Round White Gold Diamond Ladies Watch - $98,000

The Piaget Limelight Round White Gold Diamond Ladies Watch has a white dial made of mother of pearl diamond. It has an alligator bracelet with folding clasp. This large ladies watch has self-winding automatic movement and is water resistant up to a distance of 40 millimeters. The case measures 40 millimeters in diameter and 8.5 millimeters in thickness.

 

8. Piaget Polo 28 mm White Gold Pave Diamond Watch - $110,000

The Piaget Polo 28 mm White Gold Pave Diamond Watch has a diamond dial and white gold bracelet with diamonds with hidden folding clasp. This small ladies watch has precision Swiss quartz movement and is water resistant up to a distance of 30 meters. The case measures 6 millimeters in thickness. Aside from the dial and fully pave-set bracelet, the bezel is also set with diamonds.

 

7. Piaget Rectangle a L’ Ancienne XL Pink Gold Diamond Watch - $110,000

The Piaget Rectangle a L’ Ancienne XL Pink Gold Diamond Watch has a diamond dial with alligator strap and tang clasp. This extra large watch has self-winding automatic movement and is water resistant up to a distance of 50 meters. Aside from the diamond case, the watch features a power reserve indicator and retrograde seconds. It is part of Piaget’s Black Tie collection.

 

6. Piaget Polo 32 mm White Gold Pave Diamond Watch - $130,000

The Piaget Polo 32 mm White Gold Pave Diamond Watch has a pave dial and bracelet made of white gold and diamonds with folding clasp. This small watch has precision Swiss quartz movement and is water resistant up to a distance of 30 meters. The case measures 32 millimeters in diameter and is fully paved with diamonds.

 

5. Piaget Polo Chronograph Pave Diamond Watch - $130,000

The Piaget Polo Chronograph Pave Diamond Watch has a diamond dial and alligator bracelet with folding clasp. This extra large watch measures 43 millimeters in diameter and 10 millimeters in thickness. It has self-winding automatic movement, power reserve that can last up to 50 hours and water resistance up to a distance of 50 meters. Aside from the diamond case and dial, the watch also features chronograph functions.

 

4. Piaget Polo 38 mm White Gold Pave Diamond Watch - $150,000

The Piaget Polo 38 mm White Gold Pave Diamond Watch has a diamond dial and bracelet made of white gold that weighs 18 karats. The strap features a hidden folding clasp. This large watch measures 38 millimeters in diameter and 8.5 millimeters in thickness. It has self-winding automatic movement, power reserve of up to 72 hours and water resistance of up to 30 meters.  The watch also has a diamond bezel.

 

3. Piaget Polo 40 mm White Gold Diamond Watch - $160,000

The Piaget Polo 40 mm White Gold Diamond Watch has a silver dial and bracelet made of white gold that weighs 18 karats and folding clasp. This large watch measures 40 millimeters in diameter and is water resistant up to a distance of 30 meters. It has self-winding automatic movement and power reserve that can last up to 40 hours. The watch also has a diamond bezel.

 

2. Piaget Gouverneur Tourbillon Ultra Thin Watch - $185,000

The Piaget Gouverneur Tourbillon Ultra Thin Watch has a white dial color and alligator bracelet with folding clasp. This extra large watch is part of the Black Tie series of the company and measures 43 millimeters in diameter and 10.4 millimeters in thickness. It has self-winding automatic movement and is water resistant up to a distance of 30 meters. The case is made of white gold that weighs 18 karats. Other special features of this watch include its tourbillon movement, moonphase indicator, small seconds dial and transparent case back.

 

1. Piaget Emperador Temple Watch - $3.3 million

The Piaget Emperador Temple Watch is a specially commissioned watch. It actually has two watches built into a bracelet set with 350 baguette-cut diamonds. The first watch features a case with 481 brilliant-cut diamonds, 207 baguette-cut diamonds and an emerald-cut diamond on the top. The second one has a Polynesian mother of pearl dial set with 162 brilliant-cut diamonds and 11 baguette-cut diamonds. The second watch also features tourbillon movement, along with a power reserve that can last up to 40 hours.
