Jennifer Lopez Seems To Address Marital Problem Rumors By Dissing Ben Affleck In New Music Video

Highlights

  • J.Lo's new music video may reference her alleged marital problems with Ben Affleck, as fans believe the provocative video and outfit choice are a dig towards him.
  • Some fans criticized J.Lo, suggesting she is too old for this type of music video and should focus on her personal life instead.
  • Divorce rumors have surrounded Lopez and Affleck, with allegations of jealousy and tension in their relationship, possibly exacerbated by recent claims from Britney Spears about a past hookup with Affleck.

Jennifer Lopez seems to be transforming her life into art as many fans are convinced the pop star referenced her alleged marital problems with Ben Affleck in her new music video.

On Tuesday, Lopez took to her Instagram account to share a video clip from her song "Can't Get Enough," which features rapper Latto. Accompanying the post was Jennifer's caption, "Lemme get some more..."

Within the video, the duo wa depicted dancing alongside each other, clad in coordinated all-red attire. Latto delivered her verse while J.Lo showcased her moves, dropping low, twerking, and grooving to the rhythm in a striking red fur coat, a sheer top, a short mini-skirt, and red-tinted stockings.

To finish her ensemble, Lopez wore cherry red lipstick and styled her brunette locks into a sleek ponytail. She also accessorized her look with diamond cross-shaped earrings and diamond bracelets.

Fans Think J.Lo’s Provocative Video Is Suspicious

While Lopez was a sight to be seen in the video, not everyone was impressed. The comment section on the post saw various fans expressing their disapproval of J.Lo's bold outfit choice, provocative dance moves, and overall vibe.

Some people made ageist comments, suggesting Lopez was too old for this type of music video. “Too old for this Jen 54 give it to the young girls that can rap. Go home be with your kids enjoy your kids. Try to keep your marriage together don't make it a fifth husband,” one comment read.

Another person added, "J lo is getting too old for this behavior.”

A different comment added that they think Lopez should be focusing on her personal life rather than provocative videos, writing, "Take care of your husband and kids."

Overall, there was a general sense amongst the critical comments that Lopez was overcompensating with her risqué look and dance moves, potentially meant as a dig towards Affleck amidst their alleged issues.

Why Lopez And Affleck Could Be Heading Towards Divorce

Ben and Jen tied the knot in 2022, nearly two decades after rekindling their relationship after calling off their originally engagement in 2004, citing media pressure. Nevertheless, this hasn’t stopped the pair from becoming subjects of divorce rumors for months now, including allegations J.Lo has insisted on a post-nuptial agreement to protect their assets in case of a break-up.
