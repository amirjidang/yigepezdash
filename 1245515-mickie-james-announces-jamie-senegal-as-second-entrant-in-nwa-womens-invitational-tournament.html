Mickie James Announces Jamie Senegal As Second Participant In NWA Womens Invitational Tournament

 has announced that Jamie Senegal is the second participant in the NWA Women’s Invitational Tournament

James announced the news on GAW TV. The tournament will be held at NWA Empowerrr on August 28.

Senegal has worked for GCW, No Peace Underground and SHIMMER, among other promotions. James also announced that Pollo Del Mar will be at the event with Senegal.

In a tweet, Senegal commented on the news.

“I am SO grateful for @MickieJames and this opportunity she has blessed @TheGlamazonPDM and I with,” wrote Senegal.

i am SO grateful for @MickieJames and this opportunity she has blessed @TheGlamazonPDM and I with. We will see you all at @nwa #EMPOWERRR ??? https://t.co/cinTtx1WKq

James previously announced Tootie Lynn as the first entrant in the tournament. The winner of the competition will challenge for the NWA World Women’s Championship at NWA 73.

WrestleZone will provide more details as they become available.
