Angelique Kerber And Her Now Engaged Boyfriend Franco Bianco Are Pregnant With Their First Child

Angelique Kerber and her now-engaged boyfriend Franco Bianco are expecting their first child soon. Angelique announced that she will give birth to her baby in the spring of 2023.

Similarly, following the baby news, she also too a step back from the US Open, even though she really wanted to play it this year. The German tennis sensation Kerber will not compete as she took a break from the game for a few months. With the baby’s arrival, she claims that she will be ready to rule the tennis court by the time of the Olympics in 2024.

Kerber dropped the surprise news of her baby on Twitter, saying she was looking forward to the next chapter of her life and was excited about it. Similarly, she added that she wanted to play in the US Open, but made a humorous remark that it would not be fair for the opponent to play two against one, the second person being the baby.

Angelique Kerber Is Engaged To Boyfriend Franco Bianco

Kerber and her boyfriend-turned-husband Franco Bianco are about to become parents with the birth of their first child. 

The Guardian reported that Angelique revealed her pregnancy with her fiance Franco and that they would welcome him in the spring of 2023.

Bianco is an entrepreneur and the managing director of Königstein im Taunus sports. Along with that, he is also an economist and a fitness enthusiast as mentioned on his Insta bio.

The German tennis player had known him for many years and they had been friends for quite a long time before they decided to date each other. Their friendship blossomed over time, and the current couple is much happier and has a good understanding of each other.

Bianco appeared on Kerber’s Instagram account once in 2018 and has been making a frequent presence since the summer of 2021. He is actively present during her matches, cheering her on from the stands, and from the hints, it could be possible they started dating in 2018. 

Nevertheless, the former World No. 1 made the couple’s first public entrance in Melbourne in January of 2022, when she officially announced their relationship. Apart from that, Angelique is a somewhat reserved person in her personal life.

Angelique Kerber Dating Status And Relationship

Kerber and Bianco have been dating for many years and are planning to exchange their wedding vows soon. Angelique and her future husband Bianco are already engaged and are even welcoming a baby together.

Before deciding to call their relationship something else, they were longtime friends who shared many common interests.

The tennis player Kerber does not mention her partner Bianco on social media because she prefers to keep her personal life private. 

In a tweet, Kerber stated that she will take a break from playing tennis abroad for the upcoming months due to her pregnancy and she feels it’s the best choice for her. She further concluded with gratitude towards the unwavering support of her fans.

Angelique Kerber Parents Are Looking Forward To See Their Grandchild

Angelique Kerber was born to her Polish parents in Bremen on January 18, 1988. Her father, Slawomir Kerber, and her mother, Beata Kerber, were both employees at a training academy in Kiel.

From her childhood days, her parents have invested in her tennis career and provided her with all the necessary support. Her mom Beata is her manager and her dad Slawomir Kerber, a well-known tennis instructor, led the training session for her.

Growing up in an apartment at a training academy, Angelique and her sister Jessica bonded well. She began playing tennis at three and eventually joined the junior circuit.

Kerber plays left-handed despite being born right-handed.
