Christian Combs Posts Adorable Photo Of Kim Porter

Christian Combs, who goes by the moniker King Combs, commemorated his mother Kim Porter with a heartwarming Instagram post.

The photo features Porter and Christian–her son with Diddy– hugging in the park. The caption says simply “I MISSSSSS YOUUUUU!!!!” He received supportive messages from his brother Justin, family friend Aoki Lee Simmons, Ty Dolla $ign and more.

For Mother’s Day last week (May 13), Combs posted a sweet throwback of him as a baby with his mother, who passed away in late-2018 at the age of 47. Her cause of death was ruled as lobar pneumonia in January 2019.

“That day turned my world upside down. Three days before she passed, she wasn’t feeling well. She had the flu, and she sent the kids over to my house so they wouldn’t get sick,” Diddy revealed in an interview with Essence earlier this year. “One night I was checking on her, and she was like, ‘Puffy, take care of my babies.’ She actually said that to me before she died.”

Kim is survived by her children with Diddy–Christian and her daughters D’Lila and Jessie–and Quincy Brown, her son with musician Al B. Sure.
