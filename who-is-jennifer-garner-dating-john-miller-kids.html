John Miller Has Two Children From His Previous Marriage

Jennifer Garner's boyfriend John Miller is a businessman and attorney who is currently serving as the CEO of CaliBurger and its parent company CaliGroup. Before he began dating the Hollywood star in mid-2018, Miller was married to violinist Caroline Campbell from April 2005 until October 2014. Their divorce was finalized in November 2018. Together with Caroline Campbell, John Miller has two children — daughter Violet and son Quest. In October 2016, Miller and Campbell agreed to joint legal custody.

John Miller and Jennifer Garner dated from mid-2018 to early 2020. However, the two rekindled their relationship in April 2021, and they have been together ever since.

Jennifer Garner's Kids' Relationship With John Miller

Because John Miller has two kids of his own, it's not surprising that the blended family spends plenty of time together. According to a source for In Touch Weekly, the blended family is very happy together, and Garner's children like Miller. "All the kids have met him and each other, it wasn’t a big deal," the insider said. "The blending of all the families has happened at a very natural progression. They’re all just going on with their lives."

However, Garner and Miller took things slowly when it came to their children, and they didn't introduce everyone to each other immediately. According to a source for Us Weekly, "John and Jen are in similar places in their lives and can relate to raising kids after a divorce. They’re on the same page and understand where things stand." In September 2021, the insider revealed that "they have been keeping their kids separate from their relationship for a long time, but now plan to incorporate their kids more."

Jennifer Garner celebrated her 50th birthday on April 16, 2022, with a food drive party, and all the children attended the celebration. "John’s kids finally met Jen’s kids," an insider told Us Weekly. "Lots of kids and family were invited, including John’s kids, his parents, his brother and his [brother’s] wife."

"Ben’s kids, especially Seraphina and Samuel, get on with J. Lo’s twins, Emme and Max.
