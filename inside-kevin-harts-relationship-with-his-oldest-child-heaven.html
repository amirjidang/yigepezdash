Inside Kevin Hart's Relationship With HIs Oldest Child, Heaven

Actor Kevin Hart is best known for his roles in Central Intelligence, Jumanji, and Think Like a Man. With multiple awards under his belt, the 42-year-old is arguably one of the most prolific actors of his time. While his life as a Hollywood star is synonymous to movie sets, red carpet appearances, and unending interviews, Kevin's most important title and role is being a dad to his four kids, Heaven, Hendrix, Kenzo, and Kaori. And he never forgets that.

Kevin shares his two oldest kids, Heaven and Hendrix, with his ex-wife Torrei Hart. The former couple got married in 2003 and got divorced eight years later in 2011. However, despite their failed marriage, Torrei and Hart have created a healthy co-parenting environment, allowing the actor to be actively present in his kids' lives. Therefore, it is no surprise that Kevin has an incredible relationship with Heaven, who is currently 16 years. In recent years, the father-daughter duo has given us a glimpse of their relationship, and now we are bringing all of the scoop. Take a look!

7 They Are Besties

It is not very common to have your daughter as a best friend, but for Kevin Hart, he would not have it any other way. Taking to Instagram in August 2020, the actor shared a heartwarming photo of himself and Heaven while lounging on a couch. He shared the snapshot alongside a caption revealing that she drives him crazy but he loves her altogether.  "Me & my bestie....this little girl drives me insane but I wouldn’t have it any other way," Kevin proudly wrote.

6 They Play Pranks On Each Other

Kevin and Heaven seem to have cracked through the stereotype of dads having a serious relationship with their daughters as the pair can often be caught pranking each other. This is evident in their numerous spirited social media posts, including a 2019 Instagram that saw Kevin throwing a fully dressed Heaven into the pool. The actor explained that pushing his daughter into the pool was payback for a prank she pulled on him during that year's Father Day's celebration. Talk about a perfect tit for tat situation!

5 Kevin Is Not Afraid To Look Silly For Heaven

Dancing on TikTok with your daughter might seem too silly for many dads but for Kevin Hart, what's a little embarrassment compared to his love for Heaven. So when he is not pranking her, you can find Kevin dancing with his daughter. Back in 2019, the father-daughter duo released a video that showed them busting some choreographed moves to DaBaby's Bop. The video stirred up reactions from several internet users, with some wishing their dad was as cool as Kevin and many commenting on how much of an amazing dad the actor is.

4 He Is Scared Of Her Liking Boys

Hart is ready to give Heaven the whole world, but he has one big fear concerning his daughter— boys!. In a 2017 interview with E! News, the actor admitted that it was difficult dealing with the fact that a then 12-year-old Heaven already had crushes. But despite these fears, Hart noted that he did not want to scare her off and would not be instituting a no--boy-policy rule. So, Heaven can continue talking to her dad about boys for as long as she likes!

3 Kevin Spoils His Daughter

With a reported net worth of $200 million, it is only fitting that Kevin sometimes splurges money on his kids. Heaven's 16th birthday was no exception, as the actor surprised her with a luxury brand new Mercedes. A video capturing the heartwarming moment Kevin presented Heaven with the car was shared on the Shade Room's Instagram page, and fans could not stop swooning!

2 He Celebrates Her Achievements

In addition to spoiling her, Kevin also enjoys celebrating his daughter's achievements, no matter how small. When Heaven graduated from the sixth grade in 2017, the proud actor took to his Instagram page with a congratulatory post where he gushed about how far she had come. Kevin concluded by letting the 12-year-old how much he loves her while assuring her that the world is hers.

1 She Hold Him Accountable

Kevin's close bond with Heaven is not restricted to playing pranks and dancing as the teenager seemingly also holds her dad accountable when there is a need. The Jumanji actor recently revealed that after his 2017 cheating scandal, having a conversation with Heaven was the most difficult. He admitted that getting Heaven to see how sorry he was and that he was still the same person was daunting. Kevin explained:

"My daughter touched on that chord man when we and my wife went through what we went through. That whole cheating display....God damn, God damn, That was one like no other. Getting my daughter back, getting my daughter to understand that I was sorry, that I made a mistake, that was real. To understand, I'm still dad..."

And while Kevin and Heaven have seemingly gotten their relationship back on track, it is impressive to see that she wants no part in her dad's bad behavior, But with the incredible love she has for him, Heaven will no doubt always be there for her dad like he will always be there for her!
