Gabriel Macht Traded His Promising Acting Career For An Ultra Private Life, Heres Why

Highlights

  • Gabriel Macht quit acting after Suits to prioritize his family and explore other interests.
  • He felt a sense of freedom after finishing Suits and wanted to focus on his wife and kids.
  • Gabriel Macht's net worth is $8 million, which is shared with his wife Jacinda Barrett, who is also an actress.

It's rare for a celebrity to remove themselves from the spotlight. In fact, it's almost a counter-intuitive move to work hard to get your name out there, only to lose momentum by quitting the industry you're known in. Many probably look at Phoebe Cates, who quit the entertainment industry and still managed to be worth a fortune. However, this isn't the case for everyone.

When it comes to Suits star Gabriel Macht, his decision to abandon his promising career was a risk in and of itself, especially since he stopped right after the hit show Suits wrapped. Why leave a career that was just taking off? Here's what informed Macht's decision.

Why Did Gabriel Macht Quit Acting?

After filming nine seasons of the USA Network show Suits, Gabriel Macht wanted to pursue a life outside the spotlight. This decision was inspired by his wife Jacinda Barrett and his new title of father.

When asked about his thoughts on the ending of Suits in 2019, Macht told TV Insider that he felt free and was looking forward to channeling his creative energy into something else besides television.

"I have a real sense of freedom having now finished it. And I'm happy. I feel I've put some creative good into the world, and now I want to put some creative heart, more focus and energy into [being with] my wife and my family."

Alongside channeling his creative energy into supporting and being with his wife and kids, Macht wanted to explore other interests and passions that he might have stored away.

He can be a lot of fun at times, but he's got way more testosterone-driven angst and a lot of anxiety and that's just not where I operate from."

Ever since making this decision, Gabriel Macht has been tucked away from the spotlight and hasn't appeared in any film projects.
