Boy Meets World remains one of the most beloved sitcoms in television history. The show found massive success in the 90s as part of ABC’s TGIF Friday night lineup. Fans loved the series focused on the life of Cory Matthews and his journey from junior high school to college. The main characters were Cory, his best friends and his immediate family. Other characters would join and leave the show throughout the years.

We will look at the overall net worth figures of the top actors and actresses from the show. They all have a place in the hearts of Boy Meets World fans to this day. Some have continued to build success to their name in the entertainment industry while others choose to have a quiet life after the show ended. Find out which ones have accumulated the most wealth. This is the Boy Meets World cast ranked by their net worth.

Will Friedle: $500,000

The importance of Will Friedle was huge for the success of Boy Meets World. Friedle’s portrayal of Eric Matthews made him arguably the funniest character in the entire series. The progression of Eric’s character would see Friedle get wackier and wackier with his portrayal.

Life after the series has seen him Will become one of the top voice actors for animated series. Friedle has voiced iconic characters like Batman, Star-Lord and Deadpool in various shows. The net worth of Friedle is currently at $500,000 as he remains a top voice actor.

Maitland Ward: $1 million

The addition to Maitland Ward made her a later season cast regular. Boy Meets World wanted to expand the characters during the college season with Eric making new friends. Ward played Rachel McGuire as the roommate and friend of Eric and Jack.

The post-acting career of Ward has seen her transition into the adult entertainment industry. Ward is still active on social media and appears to be quite happy with her new career path. The net worth of Ward is impressive at $1 million.

Trina McGee-Davis: $1 million

Boy Meets World fans love the addition of Angela Martin to the series during the later high school years. Shawn’s character dated around with few meaningful romances until Angela came around. The beautiful story saw him find her purse with items of things that made him fall for her.

Angela and Shawn dated for years until her move overseas to spend time with her father. Trina McGee-Davis did a great job in the role and it’s the character everyone associated her with. Girl Meets World would see her appear to put an end to her character arc. McGee-Davis has a net worth of $1 million thanks to all her work.

William Russ: $2 million

William Russ played the role of Alan Matthews as the father of Cory and Eric throughout the series. The character was more important in the early seasons showing his story of balancing work and fatherhood. Russ received positive reviews for his work on the show.

The acting career of Russ continued with occasional roles in television shows and movies. Russ brought the character back for the Girl Meets World series spin-off during the holiday themed episodes. The long road in acting would earn Russ an impressive $2 million net worth at this stage in his career.

Betsy Randle: $2 million

Amy Matthews was the mother of Cory and Eric on Boy Meets World with an important role throughout the entire series. Betsy Randle did a great job in the role as a mother figure that added to the comedy while having the emotional depth to change that during important moments.

The storyline of Amy’s pregnancy complications when giving birth to the family’s youngest child was quite emotional. Randle did outstanding work in making the audience relate to the difficult story. The net worth of Randle is currently at $2 million as her acting career winds down.

Rider Strong: $3 million

Rider Strong was one of the most important actors on Boy Meets World with the role of Shawn Hunter. The friendship of Cory and Shawn became the heart of the show in its early seasons. Everyone wanted to have a cool best friend like Shawn in real life.

However, the acting skills of Strong were showcased more in the later seasons when dealing with his personal struggles of losing his absentee father and his relationship ending. Shawn’s character had a rocky ride and needed a great actor to showcase that. Strong is still in the business today and loves producing his own work with a $3 million net worth.

Matthew Lawrence: $4 million

The Lawrence brothers were quite popular in the late 90s and early 2000s. Matthew Lawrence scored arguably the biggest role of them all when playing Jack Hunter on Boy Meets World. The character was revealed as the long-lost brother of Shawn that lived with his stepfather.

Jack’s stories would see him trying to repair his broken relationship with Shawn while becoming best friends with Eric. The career of Lawrence never took off in the way most expected after Boy Meets World ended. He still built up an impressive enough resume for a $4 million net worth thanks to his career’s work.

William Daniels: $5 million

The iconic character that was Mr. Feeny became a beloved figure in the history of all television. George Feeny was the neighbor of the Matthews family along with his job as the principal/teacher/professor of the kids in their various schools.

William Daniels had a strong acting career before the role, but Mr. Feeny ensured he would be remembered for ever. The 92-year-old still makes appearances at the occasional convention. Daniels’ net worth is rather impressive at $5 million. Feeny made him the big bucks during his run on Boy Meets World.

Danielle Fishel: $8 million

Danielle Fishel was an irreplaceable member of the Boy Meets World cast. The start of her character as Topanga Lawrence saw her in a secondary role as the unique classmate of Cory. Things changed as they grew older with the love story of Cory and Topanga blossoming in high school.

Every young viewer dreamed of having a relationship with the love and bond that these two portrayed on television. Fishel has continued acting and was a huge part of the Girl Meets World spin off. Her net worth is quite high at a tremendous $8 million today.

Ben Savage: $12 million

The lead role of Cory Matthews on Boy Meets World allowed Ben Savage to get a huge role following in the footsteps of his brother Fred Savage with his role on The Wonder Years. Ben was the most important part of Boy Meets World from the first to last episode.

All the main characters essentially had their relationship to Cory as their main impact on the show. Savage continued acting in various shows and movies before returning to the Cory character on Girl Meets World. An outstanding net worth of $12 million makes Ben the wealthiest actor from the series.
