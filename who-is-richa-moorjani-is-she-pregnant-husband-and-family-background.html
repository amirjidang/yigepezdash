Who Is Richa Moorjani? Is She Pregnant? Husband And Family Background

Richa is an American actress and dancer. She is well known for her role in the Netflix Television Series Never Have I Ever.

In the show, she plays the main character’s cousin Kamala. Reportedly Richa auditioned for the role through an open casting call.

Besides that, she has worked as an actress on several TV shows, including “9-1-1” and more. For 5 years, Richa started training in different dance forms, including Bollywood, hip-hop, etc.

Richa earned a BA degree with a minor in Theatre and Dance at U.C. Davis.

Richa Moorjani Ethnicity And Parents

Richa belongs to mixed ethnicity with Indian-American ancestry.

She was born and raised in California; however, her roots come from India.

Richa Moorjani is the daughter of her parents, Ramakant Shukla and Minnie Shukla.

The actress’s love for performing arts comes from her parents, who are also performing artists. Her family’s passion for the arts led to the ignition of her desire to become an actor.
