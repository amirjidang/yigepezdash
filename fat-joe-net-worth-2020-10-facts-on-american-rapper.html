10 Facts about Fat Joe

  • Fat Joe was born on August 19th of the year 1970. He is 50 years old as of 2020. Likewise, he stands 6 feet tall in height. His real name is Joseph Antonio Cartagena.
  • According to Wikipedia, Fat Joe’s parents are Puerto Rican and Cuban. He grew in the Bronx of New York. He says that when he was growing up,  he even stole to support his family. Moreover, Fat Joe was also a bully.
  • Fat Joe started his rap music career after his brother introduced him to rap music. He first started by joining a hip hop group Diggin’ in the New Crates.
  • After quitting that group, he set up his own record label ‘Terror Squad’. This label has been successful in signing various talents like Big Pun , Tony Sunshine among many others.
  • Taking about his song and albums,  Fat Joe’s debut album was Represent back in 1993. This album was No. 1 on Billboard Hot Rap song. However, his most commercially successful song to date is Jealous One Still Envy released in 2001.
  • Fat Joe is a married man with three children. His wife’s name is Lorena. They have been together for 25 years together since 1995. She even appeared in the documentary Unsung which was about Fat Joe.
  • If we’re talking about Joe’s children, there are three of them. However only one of them is Lorena’s child. Fat Joe’s two sons Joey and Ryan are from his previous relationship. Lorena and Fat Joe have a daughter named Azirah.
  • Talking about his net worth, he has accumulated a net worth of 4 million dollars from his rap career.
  • Fat Joe has also been in movies and TV series like She’s Gotta have it, Night School, Empire, and many more. He also hosts a TV program The Fat Joe Show where celebrities drop by to discuss various topics.
  • Fat Joe is active on both Instagram and Twitter as @fatjoe.
