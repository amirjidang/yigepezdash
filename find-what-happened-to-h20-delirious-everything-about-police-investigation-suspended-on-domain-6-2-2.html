Find What Happened To H20 Delirious? Everything About Police Investigation &amp; Suspended On Twitter

YouTube personality H20 Delirious is accused of inappropriate relations with underage fans. He seemed to be in a police investigation and was suspended from Twitter. Here’s all the scoop on the case. 

H20 Delirious is a YouTuber from the North Carolina, United States. He is famous for his gaming content on YouTube. He created his channel on May 24, 2007, and mainly plays single-player games on his videos.

He is huge on the platform, with over 13.3M subscribers. Aside from YouTube, H20 is also on Twitch, where he streams gaming content live.

His real name is Jonathan. Recently, H20 has been in the media because of a scandal involving underage girls.

Police Investigation And Twitter Suspension Explained.

H20 Delirious is under fire on the internet for a recent controversy. Multiple Me To allegations surfaced involving H20.

The allegations started after he revealed that his friends pranked him by setting him up with an underaged girl on chat when he was 20. This incident took place back in 2015.

He also disclosed that the messaging contained some lewd things. It was reported that there were exchanges of sultry photos between the two. The girl, who was 17 at that time, had no idea that she was being pranked.

H20 did not mention who the two friends were. But one of the friends, BasicallyIDoWrk came forward and took responsibility by admitting it was him.

After that, other alleged victims came forward on Twitter, revealing that they were also victims of H20. The victims were being harassed and received death threats from various fake accounts. Twitter took down his accounts.

What Happened To H20 Delirious?

People speculated that H20 himself was behind the fake accounts as his account also got suspended around the same time. But H20 claimed that he got banned because of a fellow YouTuber Ohmwrecker.

When fact-checked, it turned out that H20 was suspended because of a subtweet from the first alleged victim. The tweet blew up even though the account had little to no followers.

Following that tweet, H20’s account, which was verified, was taken down. Twitter stated that the suspension of H20’s account was not temporary.

It was later revealed that the alleged victim was working together with the police. A proper investigation is being conducted regarding the matter. Further details regarding this incident will only come out after the investigation is completed.
