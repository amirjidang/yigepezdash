What Salary Does Eileen Davidson Earn On The Young And The Restless?

The Young and the Restless cast have high net worths. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Eileen Davidson has a net worth of $3 million. This is less than what some other Real Housewives stars have in the bank. And since Davidson has been acting on The Young and the Restless since the 1980s, fans might expect her net worth to be higher.

According to Tvovermind.com, The Young and the Restless salaries are lower than people would assume.

The publication reported that actors who have been consistently working on The Young and the Restless for more than five years make $1,500-$3,000 for each episode. If someone has been starring on the show for less time, they will make $700-$1,000 for each episode.

Davidson's time on The Young and the Restless is infamous as she played the same character for so long. She began playing Ashley Abbott in 1982. Does Eileen Davidson Like Starring On The Young And The Restless?

Since Eileen Davidson has been a soap opera star for several decades, it's possible to assume that she really enjoys the work. And in every interview that she has given, she sounds thrilled about the path that her career has taken her down.

In 2021, the actress was interviewed by The A.V. Club and she talked about The Young and the Restless plotlines that spoke to her the most. She mentioned that when Ashley had breast cancer, "it made women who were watching the show go in and get a mammogram. And they actually have many, many letters saying it saved their lives, which is intense. So that’s one I always I hold very near and dear to my heart, the fact that that storyline actually affected people in real time."

While the actress has starred in a few movies, from Middleton Christmas in 2020 to The House on Sorority Row in 1983, she seems to always come back to her soap opera background. And fans of The Young and the Restless and Days of Our Lives can tell that Davidson is definitely a natural in this space. She leans into the drama and gives each character her all.

In 2016, when Davidson wrote a blog post for Bravotv.com about an episode of RHOBH, she mentioned that The Young and the Restless had been on the air for 11,000 episodes. She sounded really proud of what she had accomplished on the soap opera.

Davidson wrote, "It’s been an honor to be a part of a television legacy for as long as I have. The flashbacks to my scenes from the 1980s are fantastic. What a great opportunity to relive some of my favorite Y&R moments. Little did I know, as a 22-year-old kid, that one role would change my life forever. It’s been a great ride, and it’s still going strong."
