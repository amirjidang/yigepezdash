Heather Mills Worried About Her Well-Being During The Divorce From Paul McCartney

It was a nightmare divorce for both Heather Mills and Paul McCartney. The two were married from 2002 up until 2008. Following the court settlement, McCartney was forced to fork over $48 million. As for Heather Mills, she lashed out over the treatment she faced behind the scenes during their separation. Mills told Daily Mail things got so bad, that she actually feared for her own life.

"It's been really, really stressful. The police said there's this underground movement... serious threats to your life. I'm always looking over my shoulder but because of my daughter we speak often, we are very civil to each other."

Mills continues, "When I left Paul, I said: 'Protect me from what is going to be a modern-day stoning from a certain portion of the tabloid media and I don't want a penny.'"
