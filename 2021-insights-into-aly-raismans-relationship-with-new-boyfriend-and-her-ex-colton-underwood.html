Insights Into Aly Raismans Relationship With New Boyfriend And Her Ex, Colton Underwood

Ever since Aly Raisman achieved a much-coveted feat of leading the US women’s Olympics gymnastics team to victory in two different competitions, she has become a celebrated and renowned public personality. Not just as an athlete but also as a model and sexual assault activist. Amidst all her professional accomplishments is a love life that has been a hot subject in the news, with several of her fans curious to know who two-time Olympian Aly Raisman’s boyfriend is.

A few notable names have featured in Raisman’s dating history, and in this article, we discuss in detail who she is currently dating, as well as an in-depth look at her past relationships.

Who is Aly Raisman Dating Now?

If you are expecting an official declaration of a relationship by Aly Raisman, you are unlikely to find none. The gymnast/model prefers to keep her personal life personal. However, according to the tabloid, Page Six, Aly Raisman is currently dating Vancouver Canucks player, Tim Schaller.

The two reportedly began dating in December 2018 and have remained a couple ever since. Rumors of their possible relationship were sparked when they were both seen having dinner at Empire Asian Restaurant and Lounge.

While Aly Raisman has since been known to regularly like his photos on Instagram, as well as attend multiple Bruins games, they are yet to acknowledge the relationship officially.

Things were, however, seemingly confirmed when Aly’s mother, Lynn, posted a photo of Raisman’s family with Tim Schaller on Twitter.

Aly Raisman’s boyfriend, Tim Schaller, is a professional hockey player who plays for the Vancouver Canucks in the National Hockey League.

He was born on the 16th of November, 1990, and began his career in April 2013 after the Buffalo Sabres signed him as an undrafted free agent.

Since he became a professional player, he has played for the Rochester Americans, the Boston Bruins, and the Vancouver Canucks, which he joined in the 2018/2019 season.

He once donated his bone marrow to his brother, David, who was suffering from testicular cancer and aplastic anemia. He also runs a fund, Timmyheads, which is focused on raising money for the Jimmy Fund and Boston’s Dana-Farber Cancer Institute.

The Vancouver Canucks player is a native of Merrimack, New Hampshire, and plays the center position as a hockey forward.

Aly Raisman’s Past Relationships

So far, since Aly Raisman came onto the limelight, she has only been known to date one other person, aside from her current partner.

Aly and Colton Underwood, who is known for appearing on the ABC reality show, The Bachelor, as well as playing for the Oakland Raiders, dated between 2016 and 2017.

Things began to develop between the two after Colton published a video congratulating Aly Raisman at the 2016 Rio Olympics, while also publicly asking her out.

The video expectedly went viral, and shortly after, they began to date. They met for the first time in Denver, and even though they spent a few hours together on their first date, it went well, eventually developing into a relationship.

Their relationship was picture perfect and goals until 2017 when it was announced that they were breaking up.

Why Did Aly Raisman Breakup with Colton Underwood?

At the time of the announcement, the official report from the camp of Aly Raisman and Colton was that they stopped dating because their respective schedules caused constant separation, making it challenging to maintain their relationship.

While it was believed to be a mutual breakup, Colton reportedly took it hard and even went as far as acknowledging that she was his first love and worst heartbreak.

However, according to Aly Raisman’s 2017 book, Fierce: How Competing for Myself Changed Everything, the breakup seemed to have been a little less wholesome than was earlier reported.

According to what she wrote in the book, a former NFL player treated her disrespectfully, prompting widespread suggestions that the person in question was Colton Underwood. That has neither been confirmed nor denied by Aly Raisman to date.

Since they broke up, both Aly and Colton have moved on to other romantic relationships. While Aly is currently dating Tim Schaller, Colton Underwood has dated Becca Kufrin and Tia Booth.
