Steel Cage Match Set For AEW Full Gear

Jungle Boy will battle Luchasaurus inside the confines of a steel cage at .

On the November 11 episode of AEW Rampage, “Jungle Boy” Jack Perry called out his former tag team partner and Christian Cage, as he has been feuding with the duo since the veteran turned on him in June. The fan-favorite star faced Cage at AEW All Out, but Luchasaurus brutally attacked him before the bell, paving the way for an easy victory for his new ally. Luchasaurus later scored a major victory over Jungle Boy, thanks to some interference from Cage, on the October 12 episode of AEW Dynamite.

On Friday, Jungle Boy challenged Luchasaurus to a Steel Cage Match at AEW Full Gear. He stated that Cage has cos him everything, and though the odds have been stacked against him when he has tried to get revenge, he wants to fight Luchasaurus on even ground at the pay-per-view.
