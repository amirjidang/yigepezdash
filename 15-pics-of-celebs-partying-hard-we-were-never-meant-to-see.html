We’ve all been there at one point in our lives when we have a little bit too much fun and maybe too much to drink. It happens to everyone and it can be very embarrassing, especially the next morning. Even when a non-celeb parties too hard, he/she doesn’t want a picture taken and posted to Facebook. Some pics are meant to stay hidden forever.

Celebrities have no privacy and are never safe from the prying eyes of photographers. They don’t have to worry about Facebook as much as TMZ and other paparazzi. Celebs love to party as much as anyone else but often take it too far. Partying hard all night long is one of the benefits of being famous. In fact, many celebs have built their career around going out to the club and being the life of the party. That’s until they pass out or decide to get McDonald’s. They might start the night looking perfect in their suits and dresses but by the end of the night, they’re one spectacular hot mess. Some of the celebs on this list might be a bit of a surprise. On the other hand, there are several more that will make perfect sense. Many were caught in very awkward and embarrassing moments but thought no pics existed. However, in the deep dark ends of the Internet, some of these images were uncovered. Here is a look at 15 Pics of Celebs Partying Hard That We Were Never Meant To See.

Katie Price

Katie Price first gained attention as a topless model in the British tabloid newspaper The Sun. Her alluring and seductive pics turned her into a star in her native homeland, and she took full advantage of her newfound fame. She went on to appear in the British version of Playboy, Maxim, and Esquire. She gained wider recognition after appearing on the reality series I’m a Celebrity…Get Me out of Here! She also appeared in the reality series Celebrity Big Brother. Additionally, she’s written several autobiographies and novels. She’s also released her own fragrance line and The Katie Price Lingerie Collection. Her several marriages and relationships often garner her a lot of attention. With her fame and success has come a lot of partying. On this particular night, she started off the night looking glamorous and beautiful. She ended the night needing help out of the club.

Amanda Seyfried

Amanda Seyfried started her career very early as a child. She started modeling at the age of 11. However, this pic probably wasn’t the type of photoshoot she was dreaming of. Amanda had her big break in the teen comedy Mean Girls. It was her feature film debut, as she had previously appeared in soap operas, such as All My Children and As the World Turns. She’s had a very successful career and appeared in Alpha Dog, Jennifer’s Body, and Gone. Additionally, she starred in Big Love and had a recurring role in Veronica Mars. In 2011, People magazine ranked her number one on their 25 Beauties (and Hotties) at 25 list. She’s appeared near the top of just about every list there is. She’s graced the covers of some of the most famous fashion and men’s magazines. Luckily for her, this image was never the cover of GQ.

Lady Gaga

Lady Gaga isn’t just the life of the party; she is the party. One of the obvious signs when someone is drunk is their failed attempt to look sober. Lady Gaga is clearly trying to act as normal as possible but she just can’t. Lady Gaga is known for her unique and original fashion sense. She’s quickly become one of the best-selling female artists. She had her big break after releasing the hit singles “Just Dance” and “Poker Face.” She has released numerous critically and commercially acclaimed albums, such as The Fame, Born This Way, and Artpop. It’s impossible for Gaga to go out and not be photographed. She’s one of the most famous and recognizable stars in the world. Regardless, she’d prefer if this pic was buried deep somewhere and would be gone forever. She should get a little credit for at least attempting to look sober.

Brad Pitt

Recently, Brad Pitt became single for the first time in a long time after his divorce from Angelina Jolie. Pitt and Jolie seemed like the one Hollywood couple that would make it. Clearly, he has dealt with the change in his life by partying as hard as possible. He has a certain image he likes to maintain. He’s often viewed as the charming and handsome dreamboat. However, in this pic, he’s the farthest thing from a dreamboat. Pitt has starred in several critically and commercially acclaimed films, such as Interview with a Vampire, Seven, and The Big Short. His personal life has often received as much attention as his career. He’s been in two highly publicized marriages to actresses Jennifer Aniston and Angelina Jolie. In this pic, Brad Pitt seems to be on the verge of passing out. He assumed nobody would ever see this image.

Emma Watson

Apparently, Hermione Granger likes to party from time to time. No one can blame her because Hogwarts can be stressful. Plus, that Harry Potter is always getting into trouble. Emma Watson became a worldwide celebrity after her portrayal of Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter series. The role essentially launched her career, and she went on to appear in numerous films, such as The Perks of Being a Wallflower, This is the End, and Beauty and the Beast. She is an advocate for women’s rights and the environment. She was even named UN Women Goodwill Ambassador. She is still very protective of the Hermione Granger character and image. In fact, she refuses to do nude scenes because she doesn’t want to tarnish the reputation of Hermione. Watson might think this pic will hurt the Hermione image as well. She shouldn’t care because this pic just makes her cooler.

Simon Cowell

Simon Cowell is the outspoken, controversial, and downright mean reality show judge that tells it like it is. He’s famous for his blunt and stinging insults that cut deep. The judge, Simon Cowell, would probably have a clever insult for this pic if it were any other intoxicated celeb. Simon is best known as the harsh judge on several popular reality competition series, including America’s Got Talent, The X Factor, and American Idol. He first became a household name in his native homeland. He hosted the British versions of each of the reality series. He actively works in both television and the music industry. There is a chance that Simon was completely unaware that this pic was being captured. He seems much too distracted, as he apparently can’t decide between his cigarette and beer. To quote Simon Cowell, “If I’m being honest, utterly horrendous.”

Taylor Momsen

When Taylor Momsen goes out to a club, all eyes are on her. She always looks stunning as she enters the club, making all the women envious and putting men in a daze. That’s the beginning of the night because by the end of the night, she’s a hot mess. Taylor first rose to prominence with her role as Jenny Humphrey on the teen drama Gossip Girl. As a child, she portrayed Cindy Lou Who in Dr. Seuss’ How the Grinch Stole Christmas. Currently, she is the lead singer for the band The Pretty Reckless. They’ve released three albums, including Light Me Up, Going to Hell, and Who You Selling For. For the last several years, she’s slowed down the acting and focuses on her band, The Pretty Reckless. Ironically, the pretty reckless is the perfect description of this pic. It’s the end of Taylor’s night, and she’s clearly had a good time.

Miley Cyrus

Miley Cyrus had her big break when she was cast in the Disney series Hannah Montana. Additionally, she released several albums under the Hannah Montana persona. For years, she was closely associated with the squeaky clean wholesome image of Disney. However, she later shed the teen idol image and transitioned to a more mature Hollywood star. She created an enormous amount of controversy when she seductively danced with Robin Thicke at the 2013 MTV Video Music Awards. Her provocative and risqué image was cemented with her album, Bangerz. She had everyone talking with the red-hot and tantalizing single “Wrecking Ball.” However, she became even more famous for always being the life of the party. For Miley, there is no such thing as partying too hard. She doesn’t let up and often parties all night long. In this pic, she’s having way too much fun at a party she hosted after a Britney Spears concert.

Mischa Barton

Some celebrities have difficulty with fame and can sometimes become self-destructive. It’s those types of celebs that photographers love to target. At one time, Mischa Barton was on the rise in Hollywood but she’s suffered a series of setbacks. She had her big break with her role as Marissa Cooper in the immensely popular teen drama The O.C. She began to party most nights and sometimes got out of control. Her erratic behavior started to negatively impact her career. Her career went in a bit of a decline, and her personal life began to unravel. She was busted for a DUI and was later involuntarily admitted to a psychiatric ward. She’s dealt with several obstacles and struggles over the years. Several pics show the former teen star having too much to drink and a little too much fun. In this particular pic, she clearly partied harder than necessary.

Selena Gomez & Justin Bieber

There are probably a lot of reasons why Selena Gomez doesn’t want anyone to see this pic. Selena Gomez and Justin Bieber are two of the biggest stars in the world. Selena is best known for her roles in Wizards of Waverly Place, Spring Breakers, and Neighbors 2: Sorority Rising. She also released the albums Stars Dance and Revival. Justin Bieber is a Grammy Award-winning artist and released the albums My World 2.0, Believe, and Purpose. In 2011, Justin and Selena made headlines when they showed up at an event together. They quickly became the “it” couple of the moment. For the next several years, they had an on-again, off-again relationship. They now seem to be broken up for good. When they were together, they often garnered a great deal of media attention. They were caught leaving an event where Selena seemed to be all partied out.

Kim Kardashian has been featured in numerous photoshoots and magazine covers that she wants her fans to drool over. She has no problem with fans sharing her alluring and sensual images over and over. She also expanded into other businesses, such as a fragrance line, jewelry, and purses. Her marriages to Kris Humphries and Kanye West have drawn a lot of attention. However, she’s also known for being quite the party animal. The whole Kardashian clique loves to party as hard as possible. Lindsay Lohan

Lindsay Lohan has a number of excellent movies under her name, such as Parent Trap, Freaky Friday, and Mean Girls. She has an even greater number of pics where she’s partied way too hard. Michelle Rodriguez often plays tough no-nonsense butt kickers that never back down. Turns out, when she’s partied way too hard, she’s even more dangerous.
