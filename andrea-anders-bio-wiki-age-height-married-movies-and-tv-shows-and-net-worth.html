Andrea Actress

Anders started her career in theatre in 2001. She was an understudy for Mary-Louise Parker in the Broadway production of Proof and later played the role of Elaine Robinson in The Graduate. Andrea was also featured in the Jump at the Arena Stage as well as New Doors at the Guthrie Theater

One of her first appearances on television was a commercial for Dannon that ran in the 1990s and then again around 2006. She also made an appearance on an episode of Law And Order, The Fire This Time, (2001) as teenager Emily Hoyt. Andrea is most recognized for her role on Joey as lawyer Alex Garrett and on the ensemble situation comedy The Class as Nicole Allen (née Campbell), an ex-football player’s wife. She was featured in all episodes of both Joey and The Class. The two shows were shortly postponed,

Andrea co-starred in the television series Mr. Sunshine together with creator Matthew Perry in 2011. ABC picked up the comedy as a midseason replacement for the 2010–11 television season and in May 2011, ABC postponed this series. She also appeared shortly in 2011, as a cheerleader in the music video known as Revenge of the Nerds, a rap song recorded by her former Better Off Ted co-star Malcolm Barrett under the stage name Verbal.
