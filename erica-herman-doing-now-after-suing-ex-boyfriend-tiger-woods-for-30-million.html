Inside Tiger Woods And Erica Herman's Relationship

Erica Herman was a restaurant manager at Aura Bar & Restaurant and later at Woods' Jupiter restaurant in Florida. She was born in 1984 in the USA. She grew up with her elder brother Scott in Palm Beach County, Florida and attended Santaluces High School, where she was a cheerleader.

After high school, Herman pursued higher education, attending Palm Beach State College and the University of Central Florida. In May 2008, she graduated with a bachelor's degree in legal studies.

According to Sportkeeda, Tiger Woods and Erica Herman first crossed paths in 2015 when she worked as a manager at his restaurant, The Woods Jupiter. However, their romantic relationship didn't blossom until 2017.

The public became aware of their connection when Herman was spotted wearing a "player spouse" credential at the 2017 Presidents Cup, indicating her association with Woods. Despite this revelation, it didn't necessarily confirm their relationship's seriousness.

In November 2017, Woods and Herman garnered attention when they were photographed together at the closing ceremony of the Presidents Cup in New Jersey.

Why Did Erica Herman Sue Her Ex-Boyfriend Tiger Woods?

According to USA Today, Erica Herman sued her ex-boyfriend, Tiger Woods, for two main reasons. First, she filed a lawsuit against a trust that Woods established for his residence, claiming that he tricked her into getting locked out of the house, breaking an oral agreement to let her stay there for several more years.

Herman asked for over $30 million in damages, but Woods's lawyer denied the existence of such an agreement and called Herman a jilted ex-girlfriend trying to break her non-disclosure agreement (NDA) to use her claims publicly.
